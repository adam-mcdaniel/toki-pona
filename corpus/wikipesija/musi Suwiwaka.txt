musi Suwiwaka (toki Inli la: Survivalcraft, "awen lon, pali ijo") li musi pi ilo nanpa. musi ni li sama musi Manka.
kulupu musi Kensi Lupu Keme (toki Inli la: Candy Rufus Games) li pali e musi ni.
musi la, jan musi tawa ma sin, li kama tan ma ante. ona li lon ma sin tan ni: kulupu li weka e ona, li wile ala sin e ona lon tenpo ale.
jan musi o alasa e kasi, e moku, e len sijelo, e tomo.


== musi ni li jo e musi tu ==
Suwiwaka (wan) (nasin li wile ala e mani)
Suwiwaka tu (nasin li wile ala e mani)


== ante suli mute tan musi Manka ==
tenpo mun la, soweli li wile moli e sina.
jan monsuta li lon ala. taso soweli ike li lon.
jan soweli pi tenpo mun li lon.
insa musi la, sina ken lanpan e ijo pi kulupu Suwiwaka, lon ilo "Community".
ante mute li lon.


== musi ni jo e nasin musi luka (5) ni: ==
nasin pi kama ale/moli ala
nasin pi ale pona
nasin ike
nasin pi kama sin ala
nasin tawa
