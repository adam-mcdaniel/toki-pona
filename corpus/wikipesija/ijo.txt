ijo li lon. ken la, ona li lon ala sijelo li lon lawa jan taso. ken la, ijo li tawa ala li lon ala ni.
nimi "ijo" li ken toki e ale. ona li ken lon ma sama nena ma; li ken jo ala e kon sama supa; li ken sitelen lon lipu; li ken nasin jan sama lipu lawa, anu kulupu esun anu nasin sona; li ken ijo pi nasin sewi.
