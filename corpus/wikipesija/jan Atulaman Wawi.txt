jan Atulaman Wawi (toki Intonesija la: Abdurrahman Wahid [ab.dur'rah.man wa'hid]) anu jan Kusu Tu (toki Intonesija la: Gus Dur ['gus 'dur]) li jan lawa nanpa wan pi ma Intonesija lon tenpo pini. ona li lawa lon tenpo nanpa 4 li pali sama tawa jan ale.
ona li kama tawa ma la, ona li jo e nimi Atulaman Ataki (toki Intonesija la: Abdurrahman ad-Dakhil [ab.dur'rah.man ad'da-xil]).
tenpo pini wan la, ona li lawa ala la, jan Pawalusin Jusu Apipi li lawa. ona li pini lawa la, jan Mekawasi Sukanoputuli li lawa.
