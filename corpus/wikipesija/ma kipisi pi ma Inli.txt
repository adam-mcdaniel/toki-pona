ma kipisi mute li kipisi pi ma Inli. tenpo pini mute la ma ni li open tan lawa pi jan lawa anpa. tenpo lon la nasin tu wan ni pi ma kipisi li lon ma Inli: nasin kipisi ma majuna (toki Inli: historical counties) en nasin kipisi ma pi pali musi (toki Inli: ceremonial counties) en nasin kipisi ma pi nasin lawa. nasin kipisi ma pi nasin lawa li kipisi ma Inli tawa kulupu ma lili tu ni: ma lili pi ma tomo (toki Inli: metropolitan counties) en ma lili pi ma tomo ala (toki Inli: non-metropolitan counties).
