tenpo sike nanpa 1737 la, jan Toma Pen (toki Inli la: Thomas Paine) kama lon lon ma Piten suli.
tenpo sike nanpa 1774 la, ona li tawa ma Mewika li pali e jan sitelen sona. tenpo pi utala pi ante suli pi ma Mewika la, jan Pen pali e lipu pi sona pilin pona (toki Inli la: "Common Sense") e 
lipu pi tenpo ike(?) (toki Inli la: "Crisis" papers). jan So Wasinton li lukin e lipu pi tenpo ike li pilin pona tan ni. tan ni la, kulupu utala ale pi ma Mewika li lukin e lipu pi tenpo ike.
tenpo pi utula meso la, jan Toma Pen li jan pi lawa ma pi jan mute (toki Inli la: Continental Congress).
tenpo sike nanpa 1809 la, lon ma tomo Nujo la, jan Toma Pen li moli.
