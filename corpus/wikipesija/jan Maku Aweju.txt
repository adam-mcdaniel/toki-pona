jan Maku Aweju li jan lawa pi ma Loma lon tenpo pini. lawa ona li open lon tenpo suno sike 161 li pini lon tenpo suno sike 180. kin la, ona li jan sona pi nasin Sutoki (toki Inli: Stoicism). 
ona li pali e lipu Metatajon (toki Inli: Meditations). taso la, ken la, ona li pilin ala e ni: jan ante li lukin e lipu ona.
