musi Angry Birds (anu lon toki pona: waso alasa) li musi ilo lon ilo. kulupu Lowijo (Rovio) li pali e ona lon tenpo 2009.


== musi ==
wile pi musi ni li ni: sina o kepeken e ilo pi tawa e ijo tawa soweli jaki. sina pali e ni la, soweli jaki li moli. pini la, sina alasa e soweli ale la, sina pini e musi wan.
