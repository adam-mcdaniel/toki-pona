ma Malanjan (toki Potuke la: Maranhão /ma.ɾɐ̃ˈɲɐ̃w̃/)  li ma kipisi pi ma Pasiju. ona li lon kipisi ma pi suno open sewi. ma Pijawi en ma Tokansin en ma Pala li lon poka pi ma Malanjan. sama la ma Malanjan li lon poka pi telo suli Alansi.


== ma ==


=== nasin telo ===
nasin telo Kulupi (toki Potuke la: Rio Gurupi) en nasin telo Panawipa (toki Potuke la: Rio Parnaíba) en nasin telo Tokansin (toki Potuke la: Rio Tocantins) li lon poka ma Malanjan. sama nasin telo suli lon ma Malanjan li nasin telo Tulijasu (toki Potuke la: Rio Turiaçu) en nasin telo Mejalin (toki Potuke la: Rio Mearim) en nasin telo Pintale (toki Potuke la: Rio Pindaré) en nasin telo Itapekulu (toki Potuke la: Rio Itapecuru).
