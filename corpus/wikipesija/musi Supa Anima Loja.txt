musi Supa Anima Loja (toki Inli: Super Animal Royale) li musi pi ilo sona. kulupu Pisa (toki Inli: Pixile Studios) en kulupu Motu (toki Inli: Modus Games) li pali e musi ni.


== pali musi pi musi Supa Anima Loja ==
jan pali musi lukin tan sewi. ona li pali e jan soweli Supa Anima (toki Inli: Super Animal). ona li lawa e jan soweli li utala e jan pali musi mute (63) kepeken ilo utala. ona li kama e jan nanpa wan la ni: utala en awen ale.
