kalama musi Olansin (toki Inli la: Auld Lang Syne) li kalama musi pi sin ala pi ma Sukosi. ona li kalama lon tenpo pona pi tenpo sike sin.


== nimi ==
nimi pi kalama pona ni li lon toki Inli. jan Ke Tami li toki pona e ona. 


=== nimi lon toki Inli ===
Should auld acquaintance be forgot and never brought to mind?
Should auld acquaintance be forgot and days of auld lang syne?

For auld lang syne, my dear - for auld lang syne
We'll tak a cup o' kindness yet, for days of auld lang syne


=== nimi lon toki pona (o awen e tenpo pini) ===
jan pi tenpo mi o weka tan sona anu seme?
jan en tenpo pini o weka ala weka tan sona?

o awen e tenpo pini lon sona, jan mi o.
mi moku e pona la mi awen e tenpo pini.
