jan Toli Patan (toki Inli la: Dolly Parton) anu nimi ale jan Toli Wepeka Patan (toki Inli la: Dolly Rebecca Parton) anu jan Toli Patan Sin (toki Inli la: Dolly Parton Jean) li jan pi kalama musi tan ma Mewika. ona li jan pi kalama musi Kansuwi li jan pi kalama musi Pape li pona mute tawa jan mute. jan mute pana tawa ona e nimi "jan lawa meli pi kalama musi Kansuwi" (toki Inli la: Queen of Country)
