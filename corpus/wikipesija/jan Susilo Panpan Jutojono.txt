jan Susilo Panpan Jutojono (toki Intonesija la: Susilo Bambang Yudhoyono [su'si.lo 'bam.baŋ ju.do'jo.no]) anu jan Sepeje (toki Intonesija la: SBY [ɛs'be.je]) li jan lawa nanpa wan pi ma Intonesija lon tenpo pini. ona li lawa lon tenpo nanpa 6.
