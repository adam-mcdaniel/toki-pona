ma Pajan (toki Tosi la: Freistaat Bayern /ˈbaɪ̯ɐn/, "ma pi ijo ken: Pajan", toki Lasina la: Bavaria) li ma kipisi pi ma Tosi. suli la, ona li ma nanpa wan lon ma Tosi.
jan mute pi ma Pajan li alasa e nasin sewi Katolika.
nena suli li lon anpa ma. insa la, nasin telo Tona li kipisi e ma tawa poka open pi open suno tawa poka pini pi open suno.
