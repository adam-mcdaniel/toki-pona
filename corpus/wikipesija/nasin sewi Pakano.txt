nasin sewi Pakano (toki Lasina: pāgānus) li lon kulupu pi nasin sewi majuna. ona li jo e jan sewi mute. nasin sewi Pakano sin (toki Inli: neopaganism) li lon. jan Pakano sin li pana e olin tawa jan sewi majuna mute.
nasin sewi Pakano mute li lon:

nasin sewi Asatu (Asatru)
nasin sewi Elenisin (Hellenism)
nasin sewi Kemetesin (Kemetism)
nasin sewi Tuwili (Druidry)
nasin sewi Wika (Wicca)
