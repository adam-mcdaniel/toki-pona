ilo mun Nujowison (toki Inli la: New Horizons) li ilo mun pi kulupu NASA. kepeken ona la, kulupu NASA li wile lukin e mun Puto, e mun pi mun Puto, e mun pi sike mun Kapa(?).


== tawa ==
ilo mun Nujowison li open tawa lon sike suno nanpa 2006 pi sike mun nanpa 1 pi sike nanpa 19. ona li tawa sewi tan ma tomo Ke Kenapa(?).
sike suno nanpa 2015 pi sike mun nanpa 7 pi sike nanpa 14 la, ona li tawa lon poka pi mun Puto.
sike suno nanpa 2019 pi sike mun nanpa 1 pi sike nanpa 1 la, ona li tawa lon poka pi mun Aloko.
