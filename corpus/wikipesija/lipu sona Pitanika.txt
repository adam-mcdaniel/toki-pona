lipu sona Pitanika (toki Lasina la: Encyclopædia Britannica) li lipu sona suli pi sona mute lon toki Inli. kon pi nimi Encyclopædia Britannica li "lipu sona suli pi ma Piten". lipu sona ni li tan sona suli lon toki Inli. tenpo open la, ona li pali lon tenpo sike nanpa 1768. tenpo mute la, lipu sin Pitanika li kama. tenpo ni la, lipu sin Pitanika li pana lon linluwi taso.
