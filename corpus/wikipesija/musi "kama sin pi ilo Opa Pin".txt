musi "kama sin pi ilo Opa Pin" (toki Inli la: Return of the Obra Dinn) li musi ilo li tan jan Lukasi Popi li open lon tenpo sike nanpa 2018. musi la, ilo tawa telo Opa Pin li weka lon tenpo pini li kama sin lon tenpo lon. jan musi o alasa e kama pi jan ale.


== musi ==
ilo tawa telo Opa Pin li pali lon tenpo sike nanpa 1796. ona li tawa pini lon tenpo sike nanpa 1802. ona li kama ala lon ma Olijen li lukin weka. ona li kama sin lon tenpo sike nanpa 1807. tenpo sike nanpa 1807 li tenpo lon pi musi ni. jan musi o alasa e kama pi jan ale. jan musi o kepeken ilo lili. ilo ni li ken lukin e tenpo moli pi ijo ale. jan musi o lukin e ni kepeken ilo ni: jan li moli tan jan tan monsuta tan jaki li moli ala.


== tenpo pini pi open musi ==
jan Lukasi Popi li pali e musi ni. ona li pali kin e musi "o pana e lipu sina" (toki Inli la: Papers, Please). musi ni li open lon tenpo sike nanpa 2013. jan Lukasi Popi li open e musi "kama sin pi ilo Opa Pin" pi pini ala lon tenpo sike nanpa 2016 li open e musi "kama sin pi ilo Opa Pin" pini lon tenpo sike nanpa 2018.


== tenpo kama pi open musi ==
jan mute li toki e ni: musi "kama sin pi ilo Opa Pin" li pona mute.
