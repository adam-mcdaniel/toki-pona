kulupu Kin Kisa An Ta Lisa Wisa (toki Inli la: King Gizzard And The Lizard Wizard, "waso lawa en akesi nasa") li kulupu pi kalama musi li tan ma Oselija. ona li pali e kalama musi mute li pali e kalama musi pi kule mute.


== jan ==
kulupu ni li jo e jan luka luka wan:

jan Su Makensi (Stu Mackenzie)
jan Anpo Keni-Sumi (Ambrose Kenny-Smith)
jan Sowi Waka (Joey Walker)
jan Ku Ke (Cook Craig)
jan Luka Awada (Lucas Harwood)
jan Maka Kapana (Michael Cavanagh)
tenpo sike 2010 - 2019 la, jan Ewi Mu (Eric Moore) li lon kulupu ni.
