jan Mana Kalosen (toki Nosiki sin la: Sven Magnus Øen Carlsen) li jan pali pi musi pi jan lawa moli tan ma Nosiki. ona li nanpa wan lon kulupu FIDE pi nanpa pi musi pi jan lawa moli lon tenpo sike nanpa 2011 lon tenpo mun nanpa 6 lon tenpo suno nanpa wan. nanpa ona li 2882 li nanpa wan. 


== tenpo pi jan lili ==
ona li kama lon ma tomo Tonpa (Tønsberg) lon ma Nosiki, lon tenpo sike nanpa 1990 lon tenpo mun nanpa 11 lon tenpo suno nanpa 30. mama mute ona li jan Sijun Owen li jan Enwi Apa Kalosen. tenpo sike wan la kulupu mama li lon ma tomo Epo lon ma Sumi, kama la ona mute li lon ma tomo Piwaso lon ma Pesije, tenpo sike nanpa 1998 la, ona mute li kama tawa ma Nosiki, li lon ma tomo Lometalen lon ma tomo Belun. kama la ona mute li kama tawa ma tomo Halun li lon ni. 
ona li pini e kama sona, tenpo sike la jan Kalosen li kama tawa tomo pi utala musi mute. tomo ni la ona li utala musi lon musi pi jan lawa moli, li utala musi e jan pi ma ale lon ma Elopa lon tenpo pi loje jelo lon tenpo sike nanpa 2003. ona li kama tawa tomo sona pi jan lili suli lon tomo sona pi musi utala. 


== nasin musi ==
tenpo pi ona li meli lili la. jan mute li sona e ona tan ni: ona li utala mute lon musi, ona li moku e ijo mute. 
tenpo la, ona li ante e nasin musi tawa sama jan ale. tenpo ni la ona li utala mute ala. tan ni, ona li kama e pona mute mute.
