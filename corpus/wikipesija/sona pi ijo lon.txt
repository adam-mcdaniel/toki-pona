sona pi ijo lon la jan sona li lukin e lon e ijo lon ale. ijo lon li soweli ale li kasi ale li ijo lon lili ale. sona ni la jan li wile sona e nasin open pi ijo lon e nasin moku ona e nasin unpa ona e nasin ante mute.

sona lili mute pi ijo lon li lon tan ni: sona pi ijo lon li suli a. sona soweli en sona kasi en sona pi ijo lon lili la jan li wile kama sona e sona pi kulupu ona pi ijo lon.
