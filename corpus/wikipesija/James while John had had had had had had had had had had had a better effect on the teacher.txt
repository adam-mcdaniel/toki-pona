"James while John had had had had had had had had had had had a better effect on the teacher" li kulupu nimi lon toki Inli. jan li kepeken e ona tan ni: ona li wile pana e sona pi kon mute pi nimi "had", li wile pana e sona pi namako toki (sama :,."[]!?)


== kon ==
kon ona li tan jan tu: jan Jon en jan Jen. jan pi pana sona pi toki Inli li wile e ni tan ona: ona li toki tan jan. jan ni li pilin jaki tan lete lon tenpo pini. jan Jon li sitelen e "The man had a cold" (jan li pilin jaki tan lete), ni li ike tawa jan pi pana sona. taso jan Jen li sitelen e "The man had had a cold" (tenpo pini la, jan li pilin jaki tan lete), ni li nasin pi pona nanpa wan li pona tawa jan pi pana sona.
kulupu nimi ni li jo e namako toki la ona li:

James, while John had had "had", had had "had had"; "had had" had had a better effect on the teacher. 
tenpo pini la, jan Jon li sitelen e "had", taso jan Jen li sitelen e "had had". "had had" li pona nanpa wan tawa jan pi pana sona.
