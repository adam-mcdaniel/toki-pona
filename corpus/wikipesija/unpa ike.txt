unpa ike la, jan li open e unpa tawa jan pi wile ala. jan open pi unpa ike la, ona li ken kepeken e wawa sijelo anu wawa toki. jan mute li ken ala wile pona:

jan li moku mute e telo nasa anu kasi nasa la
jan lili
jan li lape la
jan pi ike lawa
unpa ike li sama ala. nanpa wan la, mije li open e unpa ike tawa meli. mije li pali e unpa ike tawa mije ante lon tomo pi poki jan. ona li suli, li jo ala sona mute. jan poki ala la, jan poki en jan olin en jan pi kulupu mama li open suli e unpa ike.
jan li kama e unpa ike la, ona li wile ala toki e ni lon tenpo mute. tenpo lili ala la, ma lawa li wile ala lukin e jan open pi unpa ike. jan pi nasin lawa li kama sona pi unpa ike la, ona li wile sona ni: unpa ike li lon anu seme? ona li pali pi wawa mute. taso, unpa ike li pali ike tawa nasin lawa lon ma lawa mute mute.
tenpo utala la, unpa ike li suli. jo jan li kama e unpa ike lon tenpo mute.
jan li kama e unpa ike la, unpa ike li pana e pilin ike mute tawa ona. ona li ken kama jo e ike lawa. kin la, sijelo ona li ken pakala mute, li ken kama jaki lon insa unpa. jan li jo e lupa mama la, ona li ken kama mama e jan sin.
