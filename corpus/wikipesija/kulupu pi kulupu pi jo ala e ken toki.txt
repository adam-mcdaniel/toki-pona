kulupu pi kulupu pi jo ala e ken toki (toki Inli la: Unrepresented Nations and Peoples Organisation, UNPO) li kulupu pi ma mute en jan mute pi lawa ala e ona. tan ni la, ona ken ala toki lon kulupu pi ma lawa.
kulupu li wile pana e lawa tawa ma ona e jan ona, ona li ken toki tawa ma ni tawa jan ni.

ma Ase
kulupu jan Apikanse
kulupu jan Awasi
ma Katala
ma Peson
ma Po
ma tomo Sisi
ma Tawan
sina pali e lipu pi ma lon kulupu ni la o pana e lipu lon ni
