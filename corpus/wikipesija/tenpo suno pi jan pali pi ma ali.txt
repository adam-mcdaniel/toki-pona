tenpo suno pi jan pali pi ma ali (toki Inli la: International Workers' Day) anu tenpo suno pi tenpo mun nanpa luka (toki Inli la: May Day) li tenpo suno musi suli pi jan pali. ona li lon tenpo suno nanpa wan pi tenpo mun nanpa luka.


== tenpo pini ==
tenpo suno pi jan pali pi ma ali li open lon tenpo sike 1889. ona li tan tenpo ike Emake (toki Inli la: Haymarket Affair) lon ma tomo Sikako lon ma Mewika.


== tenpo ni ==
jan li musi lon tenpo suno ni lon ma mute. ma mute la, jan li wile pali ala lon tenpo suno ni. ona li suli mute tawa jan pi nasin pi pali kulupu.
