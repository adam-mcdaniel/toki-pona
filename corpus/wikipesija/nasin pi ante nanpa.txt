nasin pi ante nanpa (toki Inli la: function) li ijo lon sona nanpa. jan li pana e nanpa x tawa nasin f pi ante nanpa la f(x) li kama. jan li ken pana e nanpa lon kulupu pi nanpa lon ken pana taso, nasin pi ante nanpa li ken pana e nanpa lon kulupu pi nanpa lon ken kama taso.
