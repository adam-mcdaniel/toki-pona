kalama musi Api Lo (toki Inli la: Abbey Road, "nasin Api") li kulupu pi kalama musi. kulupu Pijete li pali e kalama musi ni. ona li pana e kalama musi ni lon tenpo sike 1969.
kalama musi Api Lo li pona mute tawa jan mute.


== kalama musi ==
