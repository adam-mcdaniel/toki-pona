ilo "telo misikeke" li lukin e sitelen lon toki pona. pakala pi nasin toki li lon la, ona o kule e nimi pakala. jan Nikola en jan Kijon li pali e lipu ni. ona li open lon tenpo suno nanpa 16 pi tenpo mun nanpa 1 pi tenpo sike nanpa mute ale mute tu wan[nnp].


== ona li ken alasa e pakala ni ==


=== pakala ===
pakala a li kama loje. pakala li sama ni: jan ale pi toki pona li lukin e sitelen ni la ona li pilin e ni: ni li pakala.

nimi pi sona ala
nimi "li" li lon monsi pi nimi mi, lon monsi pi nimi sina
nimi ni li ken ala lon tenpo nanpa tu
sitelen suli li lon open pi kulupu sitelen
nimi "e" li lon pini pi nimi pali, li toki e ni: pali ni li tawa ijo seme. sina ken ala kepeken ona lon open pi nimi "li".
tenpo mute la, jan o weka e nimi "e": "moku e kala li pona" o kama "moku kala li pona"
nimi "li" en nimi "e" li lon poka pi nimi pali ala
kon pi nimi "pi" li sama ala kon pi nimi "of" pi toki Inli. tenpo mute la, nimi tu o lon pini pi nimi "pi".
nimi ni li ken lon poka pi nimi ante ni ala
[li pi]: toki pona pi tenpo pini la ni li ken, taso toki pona lon tenpo ni la, ni li ken ala
kon pi nimi "en" li sama ala kon pi nimi "and" pi toki Inli
sina wile toki e pali ante mute, anu ijo pali ante mute la, o kepeken nimi "li" mute anu nimi "e" mute.
[musi: "poki loje lon sinpin li poki tawa" anu "suwi telo wawa kepeken namako en kule ijo kasi"] o weka e sitelen tawa “Half As Interesting”.


=== pakala ken ===
pakala ken li kama laso loje. ona li ken lon jan sin, taso tenpo ante la ona li pakala ala. toki mi tawa e tomo li toki e ijo, sama ni: mi kama e ni: tomo li tawa.

nimi ante pi nimi jan lon tenpo nanpa tu: ona mute li ken ala lon tenpo nanpa tu, taso kon ona li "I am me" li "You are you" li "They'ref them" li "my me" li "your you" li "their them" la, ni li ken.
ona li jo ala e "li" lon monsi: sina wile pana e nimi ante lon monsi la, ni li ken. taso wile sina li pana pi nimi pali la, o kepeken "ona li _"
kepeken nasa pi nimi "pi", lili nanpa wan la nimi tu li lon monsi ona.
sina wile toki e W pi X pi YZ la, o kama sona e ni: jan mute li wile ala e nasin ni, sina ken kipisi e nimi ni tawa kulupu nimi.
nimi "ala" li kama nimi pali lon tenpo lili, kon ona li "to nullify X" li "to turn X into nothingness".
sina wile toki e "is not X" la, nasin ken li "X li Y ala"

lukin lon tenpo nanpa tu: sama en lon en tan en nimi ante li kama nimi pali lon tenpo lili.
sama: nimi pali la kon ona li "to make X the same".
lon: nimi pali la kon ona li "to make X real/aware/awake/conscious".
tan: nimi pali la kon ona li "to cause X".
o lukin lon tenpo nanpa tu: nimi "tawa" ni li poki e ijo ni kepeken nimi "e" la, ni li nasa lili, kon ona li "to move/displace X".
nimi insa en nimi poka lon nimi lon ala
insa: ona li nimi pali la, ni li nasa lili, kon ona li "is the inside of X".
poka: ona li nimi pali la, ni li nasa lili, kon ona li "is the side of". ken la sina wile toki e "lon poka X".
tenpo mute la nimi poki (sama lon) o lon pini pi poki e.
tenpo mute la jan li kepeken pakala e nimi "lukin pona" (tan toki Inli: looks good), toki Inli la, kon ona li "to watch well" li "to scrutinize" li "to seek to make better". sina wile toki e "visually good" la, o kepeken "pona lukin".
lukin sama (ken la ona li tan toki Inli, looks the same) li kama nimi pali. kon pi nimi "lukin" li "to watch" li "to seek". sina wile toki e X looks the same as Y la, o kepeken "X en Y li sama lukin" anu "X li sama Y tawa lukin".
kepeken nasa pi nimi "kepeken": kon pi "kepeken jan" li "using Person" li "with person" ala. sina ken kepeken  "X en jan li ..." anu "lon poka jan"
kepeken sama ni: nimi pi ijo wan taso pi sitelen suli la nimi ante ala li lon nanpa wan. toki pona la nimi pi ijo wan taso li sama nimi kule, li kule e nimi ante lon open ona, "mi tan Kanata" o kama "mi tan ma Kanata".
"o [nimi jan]" li wile [nimi jan] e ijo, sina wile toki tawa jan Lakuse la, o toki e "o jan Lakuse" ala e "jan Lakuse o".


=== ante wile ===
ante wile li kama jelo. (lon la,)sitelen ni li jo ala e pakala, taso ona li ken kama jo e kon mute pi toki pona. ken la jan li ken pilin e ni: sitelen ni li jo e pakala.

sitelen pi wile sona li lon taso nasin pali pi sitelen pi wile sona li lon ala. sina o kepeken nasin [nimi pali] ala [nimi pali] anu anu seme
nasin X ala X la, tenpo mute la X li nimi wan taso.
X li nimi tu la, nimi lawa o tu taso nimi pi lawa ala o lon monsi pi nimi lawa nanpa tu (sina moku ala moku mute?)

nimi pali sinpin li lon wile sona la, nimi pali sinpin taso li kama tu (sina ken ala ken pali?)
mi wan anu sina wan li lon la, o pana ala e nimi "li", ante la, o pana. ("mi en sina moku" o kama "mi en sina li moku.")
ken la sina wile ante e kon pi nimi pali sinpin wan (____)
jan li wile ante e kon pi nimi pali sinpin la, ni li nasa lili. taso pana pi nimi "ala" li ken.

sina toki e "jan pi nanpa wan" la, sina ken weka e "pi", "jan nanpa wan" li nasin pona
o kama toki insa e ni: kipisi e sitelen suli tawa sitelen lili, ni li pona. tan lipu pu:
"Simplify your thoughts. Less is more."
nimi pi ijo wan taso li jo e kalama pi ken ala

nimi pi kepeken lili, jan kute li sona e ona la sina ken toki e nimi ni.
