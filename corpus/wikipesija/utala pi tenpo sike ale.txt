utala pi tenpo sike ale (toki Inli la: Hundred Years' War, toki Kanse la: Guerre de Cent Ans) li utala li ma Inli e ma Kanse lon tenpo sike nanpa 1337 tawa tenpo sike nanpa 1453. kin ni li utala insa lon ma Kanse lon tenpo insa pini. utala open la jan lawa Ewa nanpa tu wan pi ma Inli wile jo lawa pi ma Kanse. tenpo mute la utala li open li pini taso jan mute toki e utala li lon tenpo sike 116.
