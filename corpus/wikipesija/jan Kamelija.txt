jan Oja Masaja (toki Nijon la: 大箭マサヤ "Ōya Masaya") anu jan Kamelija (toki Nijon la: かめりあ "Camellia") li jan pi pali musi tan ma Nijon. ona li pali e kalama musi kepeken ilo, li pana e ona tawa musi ilo kalama. jan mute li sona e sona tan musi Sound Voltex, tan musi Hatsune Miku: Project Diva, tan musi Beatmania IIDX.
