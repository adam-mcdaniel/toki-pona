toki Ajenu (toki Ajenu la: アイヌ・イタㇰ, Ainu-itak /ainu itak/) anu toki Ajenu pi ma Okito li toki. ona li toki lon ma Okito (lon ma Nijon) lon poka pi ma Lusi. tenpo pini la, ona li toki pi kulupu jan Ajenu. tenpo ni la, toki Ajenu li toki lon poka moli (toki Inli la: endangered language).
