ilo musi sike li ilo mute. ilo sike li wawa tawa tenpo mute pi jan ale. 


== kalama musi ==

sike li pona tawa kalama musi tan ni: kalama musi li jo tenpo tan pali. kalama li tu li kepeken tenpo lili. sike li pona tawa jan kalama musi mute la kalama kulupu pona tawa jan kute li ni: lon li sike. kalama pona sin li kama pini.  

sike kalama pimeja li pona lon tenpo sike suno 1912 tawa 1991.   


== sitelen musi ==
sike supa li linja selo pi nena ala.
jan sitelen musi kepeken ilo sike la wan lon sitelen.
