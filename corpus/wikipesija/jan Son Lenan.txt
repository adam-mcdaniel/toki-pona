jan Son Lenan (toki Inli la: John Lennon) anu nimi ale jan Son Winson Lenan (toki Inli la: John Winston Lennon) en jan Son Winson Ono Lenan (toki Inli la: John Winston Ono Lennon) li jan pi kalama musi tan ma Inli. ona li kalama kepeken ilo kalama poki lon kulupu kalama Pijete. tenpo sike nanpa 1980 lon ma tomo Nujo la ona li moli tan jan Ma Tewi Saman. lon tenpo pini la kulupu Pijete li pona mute tawa jan ni. lon insa kulupu Pijete en li lon jan wan la jan Son Lenan pali e kalama musi mute.
