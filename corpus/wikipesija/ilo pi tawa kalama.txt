ilo pi tawa kalama li pana e kalama tan ijo wan tawa ijo ante lon kon. ilo ni la, jan li ken pana e kalama musi, e toki pi kama sin, e ijo ante mute.
pana kalama la, ilo pi tawa kalama li kepeken tawa nena. ilo pi sitelen weka li kepeken nasin sama. jan li open pana e ilo ni lon poka pi tenpo sike 1900. jan mute li kama jo e ilo. kulupu pi tenpo sike 1920s la, nasin kalama mute li open, li pana e kalama tawa jan mute.
