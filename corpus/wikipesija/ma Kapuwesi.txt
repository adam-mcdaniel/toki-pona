ma Kapuwesi (toki Potuke la: Cabo Verde, "ma pi nena supa lon poka telo pi kule laso kasi") li ma lon poka pi ma Apika li lon telo suli Alansi. ona li kulupu ma lon telo. 
tenpo pini la, ma Potuke li lawa e ona. pini tawa kama pi jan Potuke la, jan ala li lon ma ni. ona li kama suli lon esun pi jan jo.
