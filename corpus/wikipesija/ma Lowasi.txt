ma Lowasi (toki Lowasi la: Hrvatska) li ma lawa lon ma Elopa. tenpo pini la ona li lon ma Jukolawija. ona li kama lon kulupu ma Elopa lon tenpo sike MALLTW (2013). ma li ante e mani ona tan mani Kuna tawa mani Elo lon tenpo MAMTW (2023). 
ma Lowasi li lon poka pi ma Lowenija, pi ma Posan, pi ma Mosijo, pi ma Sopisi, pi ma Sinakola
