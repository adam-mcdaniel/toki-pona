kulupu Kana (toki Nijon la: 株式会社ガイナックス, toki Inli la: GAINAX) anu kulupu Kawinakusu li kulupu pi pali pi sitelen musi tawa. tenpo pi sike suno 1984 la, kulupu li kama lon. ma Nijon la, ona li open lon. 


== pali ==
kulupu Kana li pali e sitelen tawa musi mute. jan mute li sona e kulupu Kana tan sitelen tawa mute. taso la, sitelen tawa Nijan Senesi Ewankelijan li sona suli nanpa wan. pali ante pi kulupu Kana la, sitelen tawa Pusiki No Umi No Nasija (toki Nijon la: ふしぎの海のナディア, "jan Nasija: sona len pi telo laso", toki Inli la: Nadia, The Secret of Blue Water) li lon kin.
