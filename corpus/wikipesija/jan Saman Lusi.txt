jan Saman Lusi (Salman Rushdie) li jan pi sitelen toki. ona li kama lon ma tomo Munpi lon ma Palata, taso ona li kama tawa ma Juke.
tenpo sike nanpa 1981 la, jan li pana e ijo pona Puke tawa ona tan lipu "jan lili pi meso pi tenpo pimeja" (Midnight's Children) ona. tenpo sike nanpa 1988 la, ona li pana e lipu "sitelen pi jan Satan" (The Satanic Verses). ni li ike tawa jan mute pi nasin sewi Isilan. tan ni la, jan lawa pi ma Ilan li toki e ni: jan li ken moli e ona.
