jan Mako Sepi Sotan (toki Inli la: Michael Jeffrey Jordan) li jan pi tan ma Mewika. ona li jan pi musi sijelo ni: jan li pana e sike tawa poki kepeken luka. musi sijelo ni la, ona li lon kulupu Enpije (toki Inli la: NBA, National Basketball Association, "kulupu pi musi sijelo pi sike poki lon ma lawa").
musi sijelo ona li pona tawa jan mute tan jan Sotan. ni li open lon kulupu tenpo 1980. lipu ilo pi kulupu Enpije li toki e ni: "musi sijelo pi sike poki la, jan Mako Sotan li nanpa wan tawa jan mute".
