kulupu musi Pose Kosan Wesin (toki Inli la: Project Gotham Racing) li kulupu musi. kulupu Pisa Kijeson li pali e ona la kulupu Makoso li pana e ona. ona li tan musi Mesopoli Si Wesa lon ilo Sinka. musi suli tu tu li lon la musi lili tu wan li lon li ken musi lon ilo sona lili.
 


== kulupu musi ==


=== musi Pose Kosan Wesin ===
musi ni li kama lon tenpo sama ilo Xbox lon tepo sike 2001. musi ni la sina ken lon ma tomo tu tu ni taso: ma tomo Sanpansiko, ma tomo Lontan, ma tomo Tokijo, en ma tomo Nujo.


=== musi Pose Kosan Wesin nanpa tu ===
musi ni li kama lon tenpo sike 2003 li ken musi lon ilo Xbox.


=== musi Pose Kosan Wesin nanpa tu wan ===
musi ni li kama lon tenpo sama ilo Xbox 360 lon tenpo sike 2005.


=== musi Pose Kosan Wesin nanpa tu tu ===
musi ni la ilo pi sike tu li sin tawa kulupu musi. musi ni li kama lon tenpo sike 2007.
