lipu "tenpo pimeja luka luka ale wan" (toki Alapi la: أَلْفُ لَيْلَةٍ وَلَيْلَةٌ, sitelen Lasina la: ʾAlf Laylah wa-Laylah) li lipu pi toki musi li kepeken toki Alapi li kama lon tenpo wawa pi nasin sewi Isilan.
jan lon ma Alapi li alasa e sona li pali e ona. toki musi ona pi mute lili li tan toki nusi ante lon toki Alapi lon toki Sankita lon toki Pasi lon ma Mesopotamija.
toki musi pi mute lili lon lipu ni li lon lipu ni pi toki Alapi ala, tenpo pini la jan Antuwan Kalan pi ma Kanse li ante toki e lipu ni li kute e toki musi tan jan Antun Jusu Ana Teja (toki Alapi la: اَنْطون يوسُف حَنّا دِياب, sitelen Lasina la: Anṭūn Yūsuf Ḥannā Diyāb) la, ona li pana e toki musi ni tawa lipu ni.
