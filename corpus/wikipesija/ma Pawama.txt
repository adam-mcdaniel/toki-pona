ma Pawama li ma lawa lon kulupu Lukaja pi ma lili lon telo suli Kawipen. lili pi kulupu ma Lukaja li lon ma Pawama ala. ona li lon poka pi ma Kupa li lon poka pi ma Awisi li lon poka pi ma Lowita li lon poka pi ma Mewika.
tenpo pini la ma Juke li lawa e ona. ni li pini la, kulupu jan Lukaja li lon ma ni.
