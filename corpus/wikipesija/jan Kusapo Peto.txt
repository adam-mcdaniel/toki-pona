jan Kusapo Pansiko Peto Uleko (toki Epanja la: Gustavo Francisco Petro Urrego) li jan lawa tan ma Kolonpija. Ona li jan lawa pi ma Kolonpija nanpa 34, ante e jan Ipan Tuke lon tenpo suno 7 mun 8 sike 2022.
jan Peto li jan pi kulupu utala kute ala kulupu tenpo suno 19 mun 4 (M-19) lon tenpo sike 1977 (lon tenpo ona li tenpo sike 17), awen e lon kulupu utala ala ante pi M-19 (kulupu wan tawa nasin lawa pi jan ale pi M-19) la, lon tenpo sike 1991 pana wile e ona lili kulupu lawa pi Kolonpija la, tawa tenpo sike 1997. lon tenpo sike 2005 la jan Peto li lupa e poka ante tawa nasin lawa pi jan ale, lon tenpo sike 2006 ona pana wile e suli kulupu lawa pi Kolonpija la, tawa tenpo sike 2010.
lon tempo sike 2011 la, jan Peto li pali e ma Kolonpija anpa (nimi e lon tenpo sike 2010 tawa tenpo sike 2017 li "kulupu jan pi tawa nasin"), lon tenpo ona li jan lawa pi ma tomo Pokota, ma tomo lawa pi Kolonpija la, lon tenpo suno 1 mun 1 sike 2012 (ante e jan Kala Lope), tawa tenpo suno 19 mun 3 sike 2014 la (pini e jan Lafaje Pato), sin tenpo suno 23 mun 4 sike 2014 (ante e jan Malija Mesete Matonato), sike tenpo suno 31 mun 12 sike 2015 (pini e jan Enlike Penjalosa).
