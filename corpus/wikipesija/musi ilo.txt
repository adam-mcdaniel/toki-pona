musi ilo  li musi kepeken ilo sona. tenpo mute mute mute la, ona li kepeken sitelen tawa kin. tan ni, ona li jo e nimi "musi pi sitelen tawa" kin. ilo nanpa mute li pali tan musi taso, taso mute li ala. ken la, jan li musi e musi ilo kepeken nasin mute pi ilo sona. ken la, musi ilo li pali kepeken jan wan anu jan tu anu jan mute. ilo mute pi musi ilo li lon.
musi ilo  pi nanpa wan li kama lon tenpo 1950s lon tenpo 1960s. taso, musi ilo ni li esun ala. tan ni, jan lili li sona e ona lon tenpo ni. esun pi musi ilo li open lon tenpo 1970s. tenpo sin la, jan mute pi ma ale li musi e musi ilo.
