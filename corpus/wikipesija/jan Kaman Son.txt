jan Son Pa Lakin (toki Inli la: John Paul Larkin) anu jan Kaman Son (toki Inli la: Scatman John, "jan Jon pi kalama musi sike") (suno #13 pi mun #3 pi sike #1942 tawa suno #3 pi mun #12 pi sike #1999) li jan pi kalama musi tan ma Mewika. jan mute li kama sona e ona lon tenpo sike #1994 tan kalama musi sike ona tan kalama musi ona pi tawa musi.
