sona pali pi jan Putakola li sona pali lon sona sijelo. jan li pali e sona ni lon tenpo pini weka. kulupu mute pi tenpo pini weka li kama sona e ni li sona ala e ni: kulupu ante kin li kama sona e ni.


== nimi ==
jan Putakola anu jan Pitakola (toki Elina pi tenpo pini la: Πυθαγόρας) li jan pi sona nanpa lon ma Elina pi tenpo pini weka. tawa jan pi ma Elopa la, ona li pana e sona ni tawa jan mute. ma Sonko la, ona li sona pali Koku (toki Sonko la: 勾股定理).


== ona li lon tan seme? ==
jan li pona e lon pi sona ni kepeken nasin mute. jan lawa wan pi ma Juwese(?) li pali kin e nasin!
