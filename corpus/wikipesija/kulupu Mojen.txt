kulupu Mojen (Mojang /ˈmoʊˌdʒæŋ/) li kulupu pi ma Sensa. ona li pali e musi ilo. jan mute li sona e ona tan ni: ona li pali e musi Manka.


== tenpo pini ==
tenpo sike nanpa 2009 la, jan Mako "Nosi" Pason (Markus "Notch" Persson) li pali e musi pi ilo nanpa sin: musi Manka. ni la ona li wile pali e kulupu sin: kulupu Mojen.
tenpo sike nanpa 2014 la, kulupu Makoso li esun e kulupu Mojen kepeken mani Tala 2500000000 pi ma Mewika.


== musi tan kulupu Mojang ==

musi Kole  (Caller's Bane) - tenpo sike nanpa 2014
musi Kan en Kansi (Crown and Council) - tenpo sike nanpa 2016
musi Manka Tansen (Minecraft Dungeons) - tenpo sike nanpa 2020
musi Manka Leken (Minecraft Legends) - tenpo sike nanpa 2023
