== nanpa pi sona lon toki ==
nanpa pi sona lon toki li luka wan. ona li:
A1: sina sona e nimi suli lon toki. sina ken toki tawa jan e ijo lili.
A2: sina sona e nimi suli lon toki. sina ken toki e ijo lili tawa jan (moku, jo, jan sina.
B1: sina sona e toki lawa. sina ken toki lon ijo pi sina.
B2: sina sona e nimi mute lon ijo pi sina. sina ken kute e toki.
C1: sina sona e mute mute pi toki, sina ken kute e toki ni.  
C2: sina ken toki e toki ni ale.
