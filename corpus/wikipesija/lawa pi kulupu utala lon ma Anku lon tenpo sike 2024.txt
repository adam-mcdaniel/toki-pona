tenpo Awa nanpa 22 pi tenpo suno 3 pi  tenpo mun nanpa 12 pi tenpo sike 2024 la, jan Jun Sanjo li open e toki suli lon ilo pi sitelen tawa li toki e ni: kulupu Topulominsutan(toki Anku:더불어민주당) li pona tawa ma Soson li wile ike e kulupu lawa kepeken tenpo pi ante lawa utala. ona li open e lawa pi kulupu utala lon ma Anku lon tenpo sike 2024(toki Anku: 12.3 사태／12.3事態)
kulupu utala lawa li toki e ni:

jan li ken ala toki lon kulupu lawa li ken ala pali e ijo pi kulupu lawa.
jan li ken ala utala e nasin lawa jan li ken ala toki e ni: nasin lawa jan li ike. jan li ken ala toki e ijo pi lon ala tawa ante pilin jan.
jan li wile pana e lipu la kulupu lawa li ken toki e ken ala ken pana.
jan li ken ala pini e pali tan ni: ona li ike e kulupu.
jan pi pona sijelo o kama o pali lon tenpo Awa 48. jan li ni ala la kulupu lawa li ken ike tawa ona.
ike o tawa ala jan pi ike ala lon ma Anku.
tan ni la jan mute li pilin ike tawa jan Jun Sanjo li weka e ona tan lawa.
