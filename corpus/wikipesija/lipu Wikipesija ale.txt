lipu Wikipesija li lipu sona pi nasin Wiki. jan ale li ken lukin e ona lon ilo li ken ante e ona. ona li lon toki mute. jan mute li ante e ona li pona e ona tan mani ala. ona li open lon suno #15 pi mun #1 pi sike #2001 la ona li lipu pi sona ale pi toki Inli taso, taso ona li kama lon toki ante lon tenpo sike sama: ona pi toki Tosi en ona pi toki Katala li kama lon suno #16 pi mun #3, ona pi toki Kanse li ona lon suno #23 pi mun #3, ona pi toki Sensa li kama lon suno #23 pi mun #5.

mun #11 pi sike #2025 la, lipu Wikipesija li jo e lipu lon nasin 358. 344 ona li awen, 14 ona li pini.
