kulupu mun suli (toki Elina la: γᾰλαξίας, "nasin pi telo walo") li kulupu pi mun suli mute lon sewi pimeja. mun ona ali li tawa. kulupu mun suli mute li lon sewi pimeja. lon ma lon sewi pimeja la, kulupu mun suli li ken lon li ken lon ala.


== nimi ==
nimi ona li tan toki Elina γαλαξίας. γαλαξίας tan nimi pi kulupu nasin pi telo walo lon toki Elina "κύκλος γαλαξίας" (sike pi telo walo).
