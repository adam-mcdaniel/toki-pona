lipu mani li lipu pi kulupu mani. jan mute li kepeken lipu mani lon tenpo suno ale. lipu mani li toki e ni: jan li ken tawa e mani tan kulupu mani, taso jan li wile pana e mani ni lon tenpo kama. lipu mani li sama mani tawa lon tenpo.
lipu mani li pona tawa tawa mani. lon tenpo ni, jan li ken ala ken wile pana e mani lon tenpo sama, taso kepeken lipu mani, jan li ken pali e ni.


== nasin pi kepeken lipu mani ==
kepeken lipu mani li toki pona. jan li pali e ni kepeken ilo mani anu lon tomo esun pi jan mute.
nasin ni li lon:

o pana e lipu mani – jan li toki e lipu mani tawa jan esun anu ilo mani.
o toki e nimi pi lipu mani – lipu mute li toki e nimi sina. lon tenpo ni, jan li toki e nimi kepeken ilo mani anu nasin ante.
ilo mani li pali – ilo mani li ken toki tawa mani tan kulupu mani tawa jan.
o jo e mani tan kulupu mani – jan li ken toki tawa mani, taso mani ni li lon kulupu mani. jan wile pana e mani tan ni lon tenpo kama.


== pona en ike pi lipu mani ==
lipu mani li ken pona tawa jan. lipu mani li ken pana e mani tawa jan lon tenpo sama. ni li pona tawa jan lon tenpo pali, musi, anu ken ike mute.
taso, jan li wile sona e ike pi lipu mani. jan li ken ala pana e mani tan kulupu mani lon tenpo kama, anu kulupu mani li toki ike e jan sina tan pali pi mani ala. ni li ken awen lon tenpo mute.


== nasin pona pi kepeken lipu mani ==
o kepeken lipu mani lon tenpo suli – kepeken ona tawa nasin ni, jan li ken ala kama jo e pali ike tan mani ala.
o moli e mani tawa lon tenpo kama pona – jan wile pana e mani lon tenpo kama anu lon tenpo pini mute la, kulupu mani li toki pona e jan.
o toki e ijo ike – jan li ken toki e nasin ike lon lipu mani, li ken pali ike.


== kulupu mani li toki e ni ==
jan li wile sona: lipu mani li pali pona tawa mani, taso lon tenpo sama, jan li wile pilin pona li ken ala pali ike. lipu mani li ilo pona kepeken pilin pona.
