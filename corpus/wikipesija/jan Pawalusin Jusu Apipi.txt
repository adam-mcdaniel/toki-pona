jan Pawalusin Jusu Apipi (toki Intonesija la: Bacharuddin Jusuf Habibie [ba.ha'ru.din 'ju.suf ha'bi.bi]) li jan lawa nanpa wan pi ma Intonesija lon tenpo pini. ona li lawa lon tenpo nanpa 3 li pona e mute mani pi ma Intonesija.
tenpo pini wan la, ona li lawa ala la, jan Suwato li lawa. ona li pini lawa la, jan Atulaman Wawi li lawa.
