jan An Kesan Anasan (toki Tansi la: Hans Christian Andersen) li jan sitelen tan ma Tansi.

ona li kama lon tenpo sike nanpa 1805 li moli lon tenpo sike nanpa 1875. jan mute li sona e ona tan ni: ona li pali e lipu "len sin pi jan lawa" e lipu "jan kala lili" e lipu "waso pi ike lukin" e lipu "jan lawa pi ko lete" e lipu ante mute.
