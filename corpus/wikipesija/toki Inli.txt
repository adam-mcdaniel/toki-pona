toki Inli (toki Inli la: English /ˈɪŋ.ɡlɪʃ/) li toki suli lon ma mute. ona li toki pi kulupu Kemani. nimi ona li tan kulupu jan Enke pi ma Inli lon tenpo pini. taso nimi mute li tan toki Lasina li tan toki Noman li tan toki Elena.


== nimi ==
nimi "Inli" li tan nimi English pi toki Inli. nimi pi toki Inli li tan nimi pi kulupu Enke tan nimi *h₂enǵʰ- pi toki Palata Elopa, kon pi nimi *h₂enǵʰ- li ni: kama lili e ijo, kama lili, pilin monsuta, pilin ike. 


== sitelen lili ==
toki Inli li kepeken nasin sitelen Lasina li kepeken sitelen ni: a, b, c, d, e, f, g, h, i, j, k, l, m, n, o, p, q, r, s, t, u, v, w, x, y, z.


== kalama ==


=== kalama pini ===

jan mute li kepeken ala kalama /ʍ/ anu kalama /x/. nasin toki pi ma Sukosi la /x/ li lon nimi loch /lox/ (ma telo). nasin toki lon noka ma Mewika la /ʍ/ li lon nimi white /ʍaɪt/ (walo).


=== kalama open ===
nasin toki Inli la kalama open pi toki Inli li ante mute.


== lawa toki ==
lawa toki pi toki Inli la sama e toki Palata Elopa ante, taso ona li jo ala e nimi ilo.
nasin toki pi toki Keman ante li lon; toki Inli li jo e nimi kiwen (nimi pi ante e kalama open nimi: come / came, foot / feet) en nimi suwi (nimi pi namako e kipisi nimi lon monsi: love / loved (love + ed), chair / chairs (chair + s)).


=== nimi ijo ===
nimi ijo pi toki Inli li ante tawa nanpa en jo.


== nimi pi toki Inli ==
nimi ni ale li sitelen kepeken sitelen Lasina.

toki! = Hello! anu Hi!
nimi sina li seme? = What is your name?
sina pali seme? = What are you doing?
