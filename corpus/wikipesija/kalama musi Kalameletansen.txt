kalama musi Kalamaletansen (toki Sensa la: Caramelldansen) li kalama musi tan kulupu musi Kalamele, li kipisi open pi musi suli Supako (toki Sensa la: Supergott) tan tenpo sike 2001. jan mute li kama sona e ona tan ni: ona li kama suli lon linluwi lon tenpo 2000s. jan ante li pali sin e musi, li namako e ona. jan Sepiteke (toki Inli la: Speedycake) li pana e sitelen tawa ni: jan li tawa musi tan kalama.
kulupu Limekeleko (toki Inli la: Remixed Records) li jo e kalama musi ni. ona li ante e nimi pi kulupu pana tan kulupu Kalamele tawa kulupu meli Kalamele.


== nimi tawa ==
