pali tan pilin ante tawa lipu lawa pi jan weka tan tomo pi jan ike (toki Inli la: Anti-Extradition Law Amendment Bill Movement, toki Sonko la: 反对逃犯条例修订草案运动) li pali suli lon ma Onkon lon tenpo sike nanpa MAKLTT (2019 kepeken nasin nanpa pona) tawa tenpo sike nanpa MAM (2020 kepeken nasin nanpa pona).pali suli ni la jan lawa suli li lon ala, jan li pali e ni kepeken linluwi. jan pi pilin ante li tawa li pali ala li pana e seli li ken ala e nasin tawa pi tomo tawa li pali ante mute.


== tan ==
jan pi ma Onkon li pilin ante tawa lawa pi ma Sonko li wile ala e ni: jan lon tomo pi jan ike li tawa ma Sonko.
