jan Kulosawa Akila (toki Nijon la: 黒澤 明) li mama pi sitelen tawa tan ma Nijon. ona li jan lawa pi pali sitelen tawa nanpa mute luka luka (30). pali ona li suli tawa sitelen tawa ante mute.
sitelen tawa nanpa wan li sitelen tawa Sukata Sansilo (姿三四郎, Sugata Sanshirō) lon tenpo sike nanpa 1943. sitelen tawa suli ona li sitelen tawa Lasomon (羅生門, Rashōmon) (1950) li sitelen tawa Ikilu (生きる, Ikiru) (1952) li sitelen tawa "jan utala Samuli nanpa luka tu" (七人の侍, Shichinin no Samurai) (1954) li sitelen tawa Josinpo (用心棒, Yōjinbō) (1961) li {{nimi ijo|sitelen tawa Kakemusa|影武者, Kagemusha}|na=true} (1980) li sitelen tawa Kakemusa (影武者, Kagemusha) sitelen tawa Lan (乱, Ran) (1985).
