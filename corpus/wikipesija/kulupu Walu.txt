kulupu Walu (toki Inli la: Valve) li kulupu pali pi sitelen tawa musi anu sitelen tawa lawa. jan lawa pi kulupu Walu li jan Ke Nulu. kulupu Walu li lon ma Mewika.


== sitelen tawa musi ==
ona li pali e sitelen tawa musi mute.


== ilo musi Siteme ==
ilo musi Siteme li lipu esun pi sitelen tawa musi mute li ilo open pi sitelen tawa musi. kulupu pali ante mute pi sitelen tawa musi li ken pana e sitelen tawa musi lon ilo musi Siteme. kulupu Walu li pali e ona lon tenpo sike 2003
