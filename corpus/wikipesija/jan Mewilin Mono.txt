jan Mewilin Mono (toki Inli la: Marilyn Monroe /ˈmærəlɪn mənˈroʊ/) anu nimi pi kama lon jan Noma Sin Motensan (toki Inli la: Norma Jeane Mortenson) li jan lon sitelen tawa li jan pi sijelo pona tan ma Mewika. ona li wan tan li sitelen pi unpa tawa jan mute lon tenpo sike luka luka nanpa 1950 tawa tenpo sike luka luka nanpa 1960.
