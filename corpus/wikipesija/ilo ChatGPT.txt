ilo Satusipiti (toki Inli: ChatGPT) li sona wawa pi ilo sona  tan kulupu OpenAI. ona li kama lon tenpo sike 2022. jan li ken toki tawa ona la, ona li toki sama jan, li sona e ijo mute. taso, ona li toki e ijo mute pi lon ala kin.
toki Inli la, nimi GPT li toki e ni: Generative pre-trained transformer. kon pi nimi Generative la, ilo li ken pali e nimi sin. kon pi nimi Pretrained la, ilo li kama sona kepeken kulupu pi ijo mute. kon pi nimi Transformer la, ona li nasin e ijo.
