sitelen tawa pi jan Tawe en soweli kalama lili: lon pi ma lon telo (toki Inli: Alvin and the Chipmunks: Chipwrecked) li sitelen tawa tan tenpo sike nanpa 2011.


== toki musi ==
jan Tawe Sewi en soweli kalama lili li tawa tomo tawa telo e tenpo musi. kama la, jan Tawe li wile toki tawa lawa la, tan soweli Aluwin li pali ike. kama la, jan Tawe li sona la jan musi waso li jan Ijan Oku. tenpo suno kama, jan Tawe li ken la soweli lili li musi e musi sike tawa (toki Inli: shuffleboard). taso, soweli lili li sona la musi sike tawa li ike la, soweli Aluwin li esun e kama len kon musi e tawa pan sike suwi.
