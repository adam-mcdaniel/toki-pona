jan Isa Asimo (toki Inli la: Isaac Asimov, toki Isisi la: יצחק אזימאװ) li jan pi toki musi. ilo pi tenpo kama li lon musi ona. ona li open lon ma Losi lon tenpo sike 1920. kulupu mama ona li tawa ma Pukilin pi ma tomo Nujo lon tenpo sike 1923. 
lipu "mi, ilo" (I, Robot) la, jan Isa li toki e lawa ilo tu wan: 

ala la ilo o pakala e jan o ken e pakala jan tan pali ala.
lawa nanpa wan ala la ilo o kute e jan.
lawa tu ante ala la ilo o awen e lon ona.
