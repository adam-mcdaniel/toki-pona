jan Kaju Juliju Kesali (toki Lasina la: Gaius Julius Caesar [ˈɡaːius ˈjuːlius ˈkae̯sar]) li jan lawa pi jan utala pi ma Loma li jan lawa pi ma Loma. utala pi ma Loma en ma Kalija la jan Kesali li pakala li anpa e ma Kalija (toki Lasina la: Gallia). jan Kesali la utala insa pi ma Loma li lon. utala ni la jan Kesali li pakala li anpa li moli e jan kulupu Opima (toki Lasina la nimi Optimas) la jan Kesali li lawa e ma Loma. taso jan kulupu pi nasin lawa pi ma Loma li moli e jan Kesali kepeken ilo kipisi. lon wile moli pi jan Juliju Kesali, ona li mama e jan Okuse.
