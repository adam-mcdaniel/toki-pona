nasin ma li nasin tawa lon ma. kepeken nasin ni la, jan ale lon ma li ken lukin e nasin tawa sama.
kulupu nanpa wan pi nasin ma li jo e nasin tu tu:

nasin pi soweli lete (N)
nasin pi open suno (E)
nasin pi waso lete (S)
nasin pi pini suno (W)
kulupu insa pi nasin ma li jo e nasin tu tu:

nasin insa pi soweli lete pi open suno (NE)
nasin insa pi waso lete pi open suno (WE)
nasin insa pi waso lete pi pini suno (SW)
nasin insa pi soweli lete pi pini suno (NW)
kulupu insa pi nasin insa ma li jo e nasin luka tu wan:

nasin insa pi soweli lete pi soweli lete pi open suno (NNE)
nasin insa pi open suno pi soweli lete pi open suno (ENE)
nasin insa pi open suno pi waso lete pi open suno (ESE)
nasin insa pi waso lete pi waso lete pi open suno (SSE)
nasin insa pi waso lete pi waso lete pi pini suno (SSW)
nasin insa pi pini suno pi waso lete pi pini suno (WSW)
nasin insa pi pini suno pi soweli lete pi pini suno (WNW)
nasin insa pi soweli lete pi soweli lete pi pini suno (NNW)
