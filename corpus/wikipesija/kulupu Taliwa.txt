kulupu Taliwa (toki Inli la: Tally Hall) li kulupu pi kalama musi lon ma Mewika. sike suno 2002 la, ona li open lon ma tomo An Apo (Ann Arbor) lon ma Misikan. ona li jo e jan To Jali (Joe Hawley) e jan Supin Seki (Zubin Sedghi) e jan Antu Jojowi (Andrew Horowitz) e Wasa Peteman (Ross Federman) e Wapu Kanto (Rob Cantor). open pi kalama musi Kujanipo (Good & Evil) la, kulupu li pali ala e kalama musi sin.


== kalama musi ==
ona li pali e kalama musi mute pi poki Waka (toki Inli la: Rock). ona li pana e kulupu tu pi kalama musi ona:
tenpo sike 2005 la ona li pana e kulupu kalama Marvin's Marvelous Mechanical Museum (tomo nasa pona pi ilo pi jan Mawin).
tenpo sike 2011 la ona li pana e kulupu kalama Good & Evil (pona en ike).
