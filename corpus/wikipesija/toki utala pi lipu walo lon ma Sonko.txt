tenpo sike 2022 li pini la, jan mute lon ma Sonko li pilin ike tawa awen tan ante Omikon pi jaki Kolona.


== open ==
tenpo mun 10 la, jan Pen Seso(ante la, jan Pen Lipa) li kama jo e lipu suli lon ma palisa Sitonkija lon ma tomo Pesin, lipu la, ona li jo e sitelen "mi wile lipu pi kama lawa, wile ala e jan lawa, mi wile moku, wile ala e sona pi jaki Kolona,mi wile e wile ale, wile ala e awen
jan Si Sinpin pi lawa wawa la, o kama anpa!
taso, lipu suli li weka tan jan awen, jan Pen Lipa li kama awen tan jan awen.
tenpo mun 11 pi tenpo sike 2022 la, wawa seli li moku e tomo pi ma tomo Ulunki lon ma tomo Sinjan pi ma Sonko, lupa tomo li open, taso  jan mute li ken ala weka tan awen jaki Kolona.
tenpo sama la, lon nasin Ulumuki pi ma tomo Sane la, jan mute li wile anpa e kulupu lawa pi ma Sonko.


== tawa ==
jan mute lon ma Sonko li kama jo e lipu walo anpa sewi, ona ale li wile pini e kulupu lawa pi ma Sonko.


== pini ==
jan mute lon ma Sonko li kama jo e jaki Kolona, jan mute li moli, nanpa jan pi ma Sonko li kama lili mute.
