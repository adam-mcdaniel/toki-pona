jan Isale Kamakawijojole (toki Inli la: Israel Kamakawiwo ªole) anu jan Pata Asi (toki Inli la: Braddah IZ) anu jan Asi (toki Inli la: IZ) li jan pi kalama musi tan ma Awawi. ona li jan pi kalama musi Awawi. kalama musi nanpa wan pi ona li kalama musi "lon sewi sike kule/ma li pona mute a" (toki Inli la: Somewhere Over the Rainbow/What a Wonderful World). jan Isale Kamakawijojole li jan suli mute ike. tan ni la ona li moli lon tenpo lili.
