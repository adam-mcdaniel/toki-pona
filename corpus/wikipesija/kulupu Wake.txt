kulupu Wake (toki Inli la: Rocket) li kama tan ma tomo Losansele. sike nanpa 2021 (MAMW[nnp]) la jan Aliteja Tute, en jan Tesi Salijone, en jan Pawon Winse, en jan Kupa Latomete li wan. ni la kulupu Wake li lon. jan ona li kama poka lon tomo sona. lon tawa pi jaki Kolona la, ona li kama pali e kalama lon sike nanpa 2020 (MAM). sike nanpa 2022 la kalama ona nanpa wan li kalama “On Your Heels” (toki Inli, "lon noka sina").


= kalama ona =
kalama ona li jo e kule pi kalama musi 90s Alternative Rock, e kule Grunge, e kule Shoegaze. kulupu Te Masi Pomikin (toki Inli la: The Smashing Pumpkins), en kulupu Niwana (toki Inli la: Nirvana), en Ma Posi Walesin (toki Inli la: My Bloody Valentine) li tan e ona.


=== kulupu ===
kulupu lili "Versions of You" (toki Inli, "ante sina") — sike nanpa 2023 (MAMTW)
kulupu "R is for Rocket" (toki Inli, "W li kama tan kulupu Wake") — sike nanpa 2025 (MAML)


= jan ona =
jan Aliteja Tute — li jan lawa pi kalama uta, li kepeken ilo kalama linja anpa, li kepeken ilo kalama pi nena mute
jan Tesi Salijone — li kepeken ilo kalama linja, li kepeken ilo kalama pi nena mute, li pali e kalama
jan Pawon Winsle — li kepeken ilo kalama linja, li kepeken ilo kalama pi nena mute
jan Kupe Latomede — li kepeken ilo pi tenpo kalama
