jan Muwama Jusu Kala (toki Intonesija la: Muhammad Jusuf Kalla [mu'ham.mad 'ju.suf 'kal.la]) anu jan Seka (toki Intonesija la: JK [dʑɛ.ka]) li jan lawa nanpa tu pi ma Intonesija lon tenpo pini. ona li lawa lon tenpo nanpa 10 li lawa lon tenpo nanpa 12. lon tenpo nanpa 12 la, jan Soko Witoto li jan lawa nanpa wan.
