ma tomo Aloseta (toki Netelan la: Aalst, toki Kanse la: Alost) li lon ma Pantelen lon ma Pesije. ma Aloseta la musi Kanawale (toki Netelan la: Karnaval, toki Kanse la: Carnaval) li suli mute. nasin telo Sete (toki Netelan la: Schelde, toki Kanse la: Escaut) li tawa lon ma Aloseta. ma Aloseta la jan mute li pali e len e kasi lili kin pi telo nasa.
