tenpo kiwen sinpin (toki Inli la: Paleolithic age) li wan pi tenpo kiwen li lon tenpo sike sinpin nanpa tu ale mute mute luka luka ale  ale[nnp] tawa tenpo sike sinpin nanpa wan ale luka luka ale[nnp] tan tenpo ni. tenpo kiwen sinpin monsi la jan li kama musi li kama e kon sewi e nasin sewi e pali monsi pi moli jan. tenpo kiwen sinpin la jan li kama kulupu tan alasa moku.
