kalama musi ma ale pi pini tenpo (toki Inli la: Everywhere at the End of Time) li kulupu pi kulupu kalama musi. jan Keteka (toki Inli la: The Caretaker) li pali e ona. kulupu musi ni la, kulupu luka wan li lon. pali li open lon tenpo sike 2016 li pini lon tenpo sike 2019.


== pilin ==
kalama ona li sama kalama pi ike sijelo. jan li jo e ike ni la, lawa ona li pakala, li weka e sona ona. kalama pi kulupu nanpa wan li pona. taso kalama pi kulupu nanpa luka wan li pakala wawa sama ni: lawa li pakala tan ike sijelo.
