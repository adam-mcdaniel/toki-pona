musi Sa Te (toki Inli la: Star Trek, "tawa mun") li kulupu musi tan ma Mewika tan jan Jusin Watanpewi(?). open ona li sitelen tawa pi nimi sama(?). kulupu musi li jo mute e sitelen tawa e musi ilo e lipu toki li kama pona tawa jan mute lon ma ale.
sitelen tawa Sa Te nanpa open(?) li open e kulupu musi. sitelen li toki e jan pi tomo tawa Entapasi (toki Inli la: USS Enterprise). kulupu wan mun (toki Inli la: United Federation of Planets) li pali e tomo Entapasi tawa ni: ona li kama sona e ma sin nasa, li alasa e jan sin, li tawa wawa tawa ni: tenpo pini la, jan ala li tawa ona.
