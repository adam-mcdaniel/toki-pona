nasin lawa mama la, jan tan kulupu mama lawa li lawa. jan lawa li ken lawa e al. taso lipu lawa li ken pini e lawa pi jan lawa.


== kulupu ==
nasin lawa mama pi wawa. jan lawa li jo e wawa ali.
nasin lawa mama pi lipu lawa. lipu lawa li jo e wawa.
nasin lawa mama pi sijelo lawa. sijelo lawa li jo e wawa.
nasin lawa mama tu. jan lawa en lipu lawa li jo e wawa.
nasin lawa mama pi nasin sewi. jan lawa li lawa e ma e nasin sewi.
nasin lawa mama pi jan. jan mute li wile e jan lawa.
nasin lawa mama pi lon ala. lukin la, nasin lawa pi toki jan lon ma. taso nasin lawa mama li lon ma.
