jan Wen Ko Win (toki Wije la: Vương Quốc Vinh) anu jan Osen Wan (toki Inli la: Ocean Vuong) li pali e lipu toki e sitelen musi toki. ona li tan ma Wije li lon ma Mewika. pali ona la lipu On Earth We're Briefly Gorgeous (toki pona, "ma la mi suwi li weka") en lipu The Emperor of Gladness (toki pona, "lawa pi ma pona") li suli esun.
