lipu lawa la, pali ike tawa nasin lawa li ken ala. ona li ike tawa jan anu kulupu jan kin.
moli e jan en unpa ike li pali ike mute tawa nasin lawa lon ma lawa ale. 
jan pi nasin lawa li ken pini e ken jan pi pali ike. lon ma mute la, ona mute pi li wile kama e sona ni: jan seme li pali ike? 
jan pi pali ike li lon la, ona li kama wile e pali pi wile ala. jan pi pali ike lili li ken pana e mani tawa ma lawa, e pali pona tawa kulupu jan. jan pi pali ike mute la, ona li wile lon tomo pi poki jan. lon ma mute la, jan pi pali ike mute namako li ken moli tan lipu lawa.
