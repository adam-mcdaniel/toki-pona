musi Siweliseson (toki Inli la: Civilization) li musi ilo. mama pi musi ni li jan Si Meje (Sid Meier) li kulupu musi Piwasi (Firaxis Games). musi ni la, jan li lawa e kulupu jan lon tenpo mute suli. kulupu ni li wile tawa kama e wawa mute. kama nanpa wan la, kulupu ni li wile anpa e kulupu ante. ona li sama lili e musi supa.
musi Siweliseson nanpa wan li pali lon tenpo sike 1991. tenpo ni la, ona li nanpa luka wan pi musi suli pi musi Siweliseson.
