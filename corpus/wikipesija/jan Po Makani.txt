jan Po Makani (toki Inli la: Paul McCartney) anu nimi ale jan pona Sen Po Makani (toki Inli la: Sir James Paul McCartney) li jan pi kalama musi tan ma Inli. ona li kalama kepeken ilo kalama poki lon kulupu kalama Pijete. lon insa kulupu Pijete en li lon jan wan la jan Po Makani pali e kalama musi mute.
