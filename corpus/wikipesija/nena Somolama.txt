nena ma Somolama (toki Po la: ཇོ་མོ་གླང་མ། [t͡ɕʰo˩˧.mo˥˥ laŋ˥˥.ma˥˥]) anu nena ma Sumulama (toki Sonko la: 珠穆朗瑪) anu nena ma Sokomata (toki Nepalu la: सगरमाथा [sʌɡʌrmat̪ʰa]) anu nena ma Ewawi (toki Inli la: Mount Everest) li nena ma pi sewi nanpa wan. ona li lon kulupu nena Imalaja li lon ma Nepalu li lon ma Sonko.
