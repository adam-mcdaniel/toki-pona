toki Napejo (toki Napejo la: Naabeehó /nɑ̀ːpèːhó/, toki Inli la: Navajo /ˈnæ.və.hoʊ/, /ˈnɑ-/) anu toki Sine (toki Napejo la: Diné /tɪ̀nɛ́/) li toki pi kulupu jan nanpa wan lon ma Amelika. ona li toki lon ma Ewisona lon ma Juta lon ma Kolowato lon ma Numesiko lon ma Mewika. toki ni li tan kulupu toki Natene.
