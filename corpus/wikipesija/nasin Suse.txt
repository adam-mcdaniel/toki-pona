nasin Suse (toki Soson la: 주체사상／主體思想 Juche Sasang)li nasin lawa pi ma Soson li nasin ante pi nasin loje.


== jo pi nasin Suse ==
kon pi nasin Suse: jan li jan lawa pi utala en pali li wawa tawa utala en pali.jan li jan lawa jan li wawa tawa pali e tenpo kama jan.
kon tan pi nasin Suse: kon tan pi nasin Suse li toki e ni: sona lon en nasin en pi ali tan jan,jan li pali e nasin utala jan.
kon suli taso sitelen lili: jan li pali e tenpo kama jan.
tan pi nasin Suse lon sona sona: nasin Suse li toki e nasin pi ali en jan.jan li lawa e ali.
tan pi nasin Suse lon tenpo pini pi kulupu jan: jan li sijelo suli pi tenpo pini.
kon tan pi pali lon tenpo pini pi kulupu jan: tenpo pini jan li pali e ni:jan li kama kepeken ala e ante li wan.
kon insa pi pali lon tenpo pini pi kulupu jan: pali lon tenpo pini pi kulupu jan li ni: pali pi kulupu jan.
wawa pi pali lon tenpo pini pi kulupu jan: jan li kepeken ala ante li wan li suli tawa utala tan pona.
