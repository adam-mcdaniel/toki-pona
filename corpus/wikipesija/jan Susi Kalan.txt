jan Susi Kalan (toki Inli la: Judy Garland) anu jan Panse Ese Kan (toki Inli la: Frances Ethel Gumm) li jan lon sitelen tawa li jan pi kalama musi tan ma Mewika. jan mute ken ona tan sitelen tawa "jan Osu pi wawa nasa". ona li jan Towasi Keli lon sitelen tawa ni. ona toki kalama musi "lon sewi sike kule". kalama musi ni li pona tawa jan mute.
