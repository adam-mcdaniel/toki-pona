sona pi lawa jan anu sona pilin (toki Elena la: Ψυχολογία, "sona pi kon jan") li nasin sona pi nasin jan. ona li sona e toki insa e pilin e nasin jan. sona ni li ken pona e lawa jan.


== nasin ==


=== nasin kon ===
jan Simun Poli (toki Tosi la: Sigmund Freud) li mama e nasin kon.
nasin kon li kipisi e lawa: kon len (toki Tosi la: Es, "ni") en kon jan (toki Tosi la: Ich, "mi") en kon insa (toki Tosi la: Über-Ich, "sewi mi").


==== kon len ====
kon len li ante ala li awen. wile li lawa e kon len.


=== nasin pi nasin jan ===
jan Puku Peteli Kine (toki Inli la: Burrhus Frederic Skinner) li mama e nasin pi nasin jan.


=== nasin pi pona jan ===
jan Apama Malo (toki Inli la: Abraham Maslow) li mama e nasin pi pona jan.
