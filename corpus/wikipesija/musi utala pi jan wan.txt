musi utala pi jan wan (toki Inli: first-person shooter) li kulupu pi musi ilo. jan li kepeken e ilo utala wawa tan kulupu musi ni. jan li lukin sama oko pi jan lon ala. mama pi kulupu musi ni li musi Wolensan 3D (Wolfenstein 3D). musi Tun (Doom) li musi suli mute. tan ni la, nimi ante pi kulupu musi ni li "sama pi musi Tun" (Doom clone).
musi mute utala pi jan wan li lon:

musi Wolensan 3D (Wolfenstein 3D)
musi Tun (Doom)
musi Kuweke (Quake)
musi pi Kotena 007 (Goldeneye 007)
musi pi jan Tu Nuken 3D (Duke Nukem 3D)
musi pi ma utala (Battlefield)
musi Kolotusi (Call of Duty)
musi pi kalama weka (Far Cry)
