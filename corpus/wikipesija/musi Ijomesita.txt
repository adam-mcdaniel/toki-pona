musi Ijomesita (toki Inli la: Geometry Dash, "tawa leko") li musi ilo pi jan wan. jan RobTop (anu jan Robert Topala) li pali e musi ni. musi li kama lon tenpo mute ale luka luka tu wan (2013). musi la, jan o weka e ijo ike. musi li jo e ma mute luka wan (26). jan RobTop li pali e ma ni. ma mute tu (22) la, jan li ken ala ante e nasin ona. ma tu tu (4) la, jan li ken ante e nasin ona. jan li ken pali e ma ona. jan li ken musi e ma pi jan ante kepeken linluwi.
