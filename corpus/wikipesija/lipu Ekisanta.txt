lipu Ekisanta (toki Inli la: xHamster) li lipu pi sitelen unpa pi mani ala li lipu linluwi wawa nanpa 57 lon lipu linluwi ale.


== sitelen esun ike ==
jan li sona e ni: sitelen esun ike li lon lipu ni li pali ike lon ilo sona.ijo pi pali ike li pakala e lipu ni.


== ma pi kepeken ala ==
tan lawa pi sitelen unpa la, ma Palata en ma Lusi en ma Alapija en ma Sonko en ma Tuki en ma Intonesija li ala e ken kepeken.
