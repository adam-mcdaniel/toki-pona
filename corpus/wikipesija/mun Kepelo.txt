mun Kepelo (toki Inli la: Kerberos) li mun pi mun Puto. mun ale pi mun Puto la, ona li lili nanpa tu. tenpo suno nanpa 28 pi tenpo mun nanpa 6 pi tenpo sike nanpa 2011 la, jan Ma Sowata (toki Inli la: Mark Showalter) en kulupu ona li lukin e mun Kepelo lon tenpo nanpa wan, kepeken ilo lukin Apo(?).
sijelo pi mun Kepelo li jo e sike tu. tan sike tu ni li lon la, jan sona li toki e ni: "ken la, ijo lili tu li kama wan li kama mun Kepelo lon tenpo pini".
