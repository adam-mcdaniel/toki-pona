toki Sinakola (toki Sinakola: црногорски anu crnogorski) li ante toki e tan toki Sopisi-Lowasi li kepeken e  kulupu Sinakola. lon sike suno nanpa 2006 e ma Sinakola li sin, kama la lon sike suno nanpa 2007 e ma Sinakola lawa toki e toki Sinakola li lawa toki pi ma Sinakola.


== sitelen ==
toki Sinakola li kepeken e sitelen Kililisa e sitelen Lasina.
