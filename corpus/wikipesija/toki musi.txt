toki musi la, nasin tu suli li toki pi lon ala li toki lon.


== sitelen pi toki musi ==

sitelen pi toki musi li toki musi lon sitelen toki. sitelen li ken nasin pi awen toki musi. lon kulupu jan pi sitelen toki la, sitelen pi toki musi li kama suli mute tawa nasin kulupu. ona li suli tawa musi tawa sona tawa nasin sewi tawa nasin lawa. ona li nasin mute pi sitelen pi toki musi. nasin suli pi sitelen musi li sitelen pi kalama musi li sitelen toki pi pali musi li sitelen pi sama toki (sitelen Posa li nasin ali ante pi sitelen toki musi).
tenpo mute pini la, jan mute li sitelen e toki musi kepeken kiwen. tenpo ni la, jan mute li kepeken lipu toki anu ilo sona.
