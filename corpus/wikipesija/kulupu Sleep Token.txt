kulupu pi sitelen lape (toki Inli la: Sleep Token [ˈsliːp ˈtəʊkən) li kulupu pi kalama musi. ona li kama tan pi ma tomo Lontan lon ma Inli. 


== tenpo pini ==
tenpo sike nanpa 2016 la, kulupu pi sitelen lape li kama. ale li sona e jan kulupu. jan kulupu li jule. jan pi kalama musi pi kepeken uta li jan poki (Vessel [ˈvɛs.l̩]). jan poki li nimi kulupu pi sona taso. sona pi kulupu ni li jan sewi lape. jan sewi lape li lon insa oni pi jan poki la, kulupu olin tan kalama musi.
kalama musi nanpa wan pi kulupu ni li lipu lili suli "One" li kalama musi "Thread the Needle" (wan, o pali e ilo tawa len).
tenpo sike poka la, kulupu pi sitelen lape li pana e lipu lili suli nanpa tu "Two" e kalama musi "Calcutta" e kalama musi "Nazareth" e kalama musi "Jericho" (tu, ma tomo Kolokata, ma tomo Nasala). tenpo sike sama la, ona li pana e kalama musi "Hey Ya!" e kalama musi "Jaws" e kalama musi "The Way That You Were" (sina o, uta monsuta，nasin sina pi tenpo pini). "Hey Ya!" li kalama musi tan kulupu OutKast (jan lon ete kulupu). kalama musi "Hey Ya!" en kalama musi "Jaws" en kalama musi "The Way That You Were" li weka lipu pi kalama musi.
meso pi tenpo sike nanpa 2019 la, kulupu pi sitelen lape li pana e kalama musi  "The Night Does Not Belong To God" (tenpo pimeja li jo ala e jan sewi). kalama musi li open pi lipu suli nanpa wan "Sundowning" (lawa ike pi tenpo pimeja. kulupu pi sitelen lape li pana e musi ni insa kulupu mani Spinefarm Records (kulupu musi mani Panipamu Lekotu). tenpo sike ni la kulupu pi sitelen lape li pana e musi sin mute. musi sin li lon insa lipu suli  "Sundowning". tenpo sike poka la，kulupu pi sitelen lape li pana e lipu "Sundowning" namako. lipu "Sundowning" namako li jo e kalama musi sin.
tenpo sike nanpa 2021 la, kulupu pi sitelen lape li pana e kalama musi "The Love You Want" e kalama musi "Alkaline" e kalama musi "Fall For Me" (olin pi wile sina, ijo pi walo ale pi pimeja ale, sina o olin e mi)。kalama musi li open pi lipu suli "This Place Will Be Your Tomb" (tenpo pi sina moli la，sina lape lon ni). kulupu li pali kan jan Joku Liwa (toki Inli la: George Lever [ˈdʒɔːɹdʒ ˈli.və). lipu suli "This Place Will Be Your Tomb" li pona mute tawa lipu Loudwire (lipu Latuwewi)。
open pi tenpo sike nanpa 2023 la，kulupu pi sitelen lape li pana e kalama musi "Chokehold" e kalama musi "The Summoning" e kalama musi "Granite" e kalama musi "Aqua Regia" e kalama musi "DYWTYLM" (o kon ale，kama sewi，kiwen，telo jaki sewi，sina wile ala wile olin e mi?). kalama musi li tawa lipu suli kama "Take Me Back To Eden" (o lanpan e mi. mi wile lon ma sewi Iten).


== nasin pi kalama musi ==
kulupu pi sitelen lape li wan tawa jan mute. nasin ona li ante mute. nasin li kalama musi wawa li kalama musi lili li kalama musi namako.
