kulupu Kupu en jan Kasa (toki Tosi la: Krupp und Krause) li sitelen tawa lon ma Tosi pi open suno li jo e kipisi luka.kipisi nanpa wan li pana e sona lon kama wawa pali lon ma Tosi lon sinpin pi tenpo sike nanpa LLLTTAMLLTW[nnp], lili li ante tan lipu kulupu Kupu en jan Kasa (toki Tosi la: Krupp und Krause) pi jan Ka Anki En.sitelen tawa ni la jan li kama lukin e ma pali pi jan pali lon sinpin utala e ken pali (pona) pi ma Tosi pi open suno li suli e tenpo pi kama wawa pali tawa tenpo sike MMM li pana e sitelen suli pi sama ala.


== jo ==
sitelen tawa ni li pana e lon pi jan Pe Kasa (Fred Krause), ona li pali lon kulupu Kupu.jan Pe Kasa li sona e nasin loje la, ona li lon kulupu pi nasin loje li sona e ni: kulupu Kupu li lanpan e mani mute tan esun utala.
jan Pe Kasa li pilin pona tawa utala pi jan pali tan pilin ante lon ma tomo Esun.jan li suli e kon ona taso ona li wan lon tenpo pini pi ma Tosi pi open suno.pini la ona li kama pali lon kulupu Kupu li pilin ante tawa ni: jan lawa ona li wile lanpan pi ken ala e ijo pi ma Sesesele.ona li pali tan kulupu jan pi nasin loje li kama jan lawa pi kulupu Kupu.
