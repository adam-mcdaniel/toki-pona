toki Kasa (toki Kasa la: qazaq≈üa) li toki pi ma Kasa. kin la, jan mute lon ma Kasa li kepeken toki Losi.
toki Kasa li toki pi ma mute. ona li toki lawa pi ma Kasa li toki lon ma Sonko, lon ma Monko, lon ma Lusi, lon ma Kilisa, lon ma Opeki, lon ma mute ante. ante mute pi toki Kasa li lon. sitelen la ona ale li sama mute. taso toki kalama la ona li ante mute.
