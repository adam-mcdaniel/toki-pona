tenpo sike 2024 la, jan li pana e wile ona tawa jan lawa pi ma Mewika. jan Tana Tan pi kulupu Wepalikan li anpa e jan Kamala Ewi pi kulupu Temokasi, li kama jan lawa sin. 
insa pi tenpo sike 2017 pi tenpo sike 2021 la jan Tana Tan li jan lawa pi ma Mewika. taso, ona li kama anpa tan jan So Patan lon pana wile pi tenpo sike 2020. jan So Patan li wile e tenpo lawa sin. taso, ona li pini wile. jan Kamala Ewi li kama jan ken sin pi jan lawa lon kulupu Temokasi.
