jan Mewika Apika (toki Inli la: African Americans) li kulupu jan pimeja lon ma Mewika. ona mute li jo e mama suli anu sin tan ma Apika lon anpa pi ma suli Asara (toki Alapi la: صحراء sˤaħ.raːʔ). jan Mewika Apika li jan nanpa tu lon ma Mewika.


== tenpo pini ==
tenpo sike ale 16 la, ma Mewika li kama jo e jan Apika. lon Apika la, jan esun jan li lon. ona mute li pana e jan Apika mute tawa jan esun tan ma Elopa. ona mute li tawa e jan Apika lon ma Mewika. 
lon ni la, jan Apika li pali lon ma kasi. ona mute li jo ala e wawa e ken. jan nanpa lili mute li kama wawa li tawa tan jan esun ona.
