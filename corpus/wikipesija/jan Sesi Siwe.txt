jan Sesi Siwe (toki Inli: J.G. Thirlwell) li jan pi kalama musi pi ma Oselija. ona li pali e kalama musi pali. kalama musi ona li monsuta li suli kalama li nasa. jan li pana e nimi pi jan lili insa (toki Inli: Foetus) tawa ona. ona li pali e kalama musi pi sitelen pi jan Wensa (toki Inli: The Venture Bros.) e kalama musi pi sitelen pi jan Asa (toki Inli: Archer).
