lipu su li kulupu lipu pi jan Sonja. sike suno nanpa 2024 la sike mun nanpa tu la suno nanpa tu wan la ona li pana e lipu nanpa wan. ona li kepeken sitelen pona. tenpo lon la lipu wan taso li lon. taso jan Sonja li wile pali e lipu mute lon tenpo kama.


== nimi ==

tenpo pini la jan Sonja li toki e ni: ona li wile kepeken nimi "su". nimi ni ante li nimi "ju" li nimi "u" li nimi "lu" li nimi "nu".


== lipu nanpa wan ==
nimi lawa pi lipu nanpa wan li "jan Osu pi wawa nasa". ona li open tan jan Liman Pa Pon (Lyman Frank Baum) lon toki Inli. toki Inli la, nimi lawa ona li "The Wonderful Wizard of Oz". jan li ken esun e lipu pi jan Osu lon lipu ni.


== lipu nanpa tu ==
lipu su nanpa tu li toki lon olin lon kulupu pi toki Epelanto pi toki pona pi toki Kosa pi ante mute. sina ken kama jo e ona lon ni. o lukin e lipu sunosama kin. ona li pana lon tenpo suno nanpa 10 pi tenpo mun nanpa 7 pi tenpo sike nanpa 2025.


== lipu nanpa tu wan ==

lipu su nanpa tu wan li lipu lon sitelen pona. sina ken lukin e toki pi lukin lon ni.
