ma Amapa (toki Potuke la: Amapá /a.maˈpa/) li ma kipisi pi ma Pasiju. ona li lon kipisi ma sewi. ma Pasiju lon la ma Pala li lon poka pi ma Amapa. ante ma lon poka pi ona li ma Kijanakanse en ma Suliname. sama la ma Amapa li lon poka pi telo suli Alansi.


== ma ==


=== nasin telo ===
nasin telo Amasona (toki Potuke la: Rio Amazonas) en nasin telo Sali (toki Potuke la: Rio Jari) en nasin telo Ojapoki (toki Potuke la: Rio Oiapoque) li lon poka ma Amapa. sama la nasin telo Alakali (toki Potuke la: Rio Araguari) li lon ma Amapa.
