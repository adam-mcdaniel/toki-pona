jan Tala Kekoli Okoma (toki Inli la: Tyler Gregory Okonma) li jan pi kalama musi tan ma Mewika. pali la ona li jan mama Tala (toki Inli la: Tyler, the Creator). ona li suli tawa kalama musi Ipope ante lon kulupu tenpo pi tenpo sike nanpa 2010. jan Tala li open kama suli lon tenpo sike nanpa 2009. tenpo ni la, ona li kama lawa pi kulupu kalama Odd Future. jan Tala li lon sitelen tawa Loiter Squad.
