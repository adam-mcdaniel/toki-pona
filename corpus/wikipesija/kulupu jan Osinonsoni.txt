kulupu jan Osinonsoni (Haudenosaunee) li kulupu  pi kulupu jan mute. tenpo open la, ona li jo e kulupu Mohawk, e kulupu Oneida, e kulupu Onondaga, e kulupu Cayuga, e kulupu Seneca. tenpo kama la, kulupu Tuscarora li kama. kulupu Osinonsoni li lon kulupu jan nanpa wan pi ma Amelika.
tenpo sin la, jan pi kulupu Osinonsoni li lon ma Kanata, lon ma Mewika.
