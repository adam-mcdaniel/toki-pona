kulupu Matana (toki Inli la: McDonald's /məkˈdɒnəldz/) li kulupu esun suli tan ma Mewika. ona li pali e moku pi tenpo lili.
jan Wisa Matana (toki Inli la: Richard McDonald) en jan Mowi Matana (toki Inli la: Maurice McDonald) li mama e moku tomo nanpa wan lon tenpo sike nanpa 1940. taso tenpo sike nanpa 1955 la jan We Ko (toki Inli la: Ray Kroc) li pali e kulupu pi ni. tenpo ni la, jan lawa ona li jan Ki Kensinki (toki Inli la: Chris Kempczinski).
sitelen lawa pi kulupu Matana li kipisi sike jelo tu (toki Inli la: Golden Arches). jan sitelen pi kulupu Matana li jan Wana Matana (toki Inli la: Ronald McDonald)
