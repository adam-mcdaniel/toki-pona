jan Pan Sinata (toki Inli la: Frank Sinatra) anu nimi ale jan Pansi Ape Sinata (toki Inli la: Francis Albert Sinatra) anu nimi lili jan lawa pi kulupu lawa (toki Inli la: Chairman of the Board) anu nimi lili jan pi tenpo mute pi lukin laso (toki Inli la: Ol' Blue Eyes) li jan pi kalama musi li jan lon sitelen tawa tan ma Mewika. kulupu mama ona li tan ma Italija. ona li jan pi kalama uta musi pona mute tawa jan mute. tenpo sike nanpa 1998 la ona li moli tan utala pilin.
