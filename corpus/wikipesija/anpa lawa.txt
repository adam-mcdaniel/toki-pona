anpa lawa (toki Tawi: ไสยศาสตร์; toki Sonko: 降头术, sitelen Pin In: jiàng tóu shù; toki Inli: Tame Head) li nasin pi wawa nasa lon ma Sonko lon ma Tawi lon ma Kanpusi lon ma Lajo lon ma Mijama lon ma Malasija lon ma Intonesija.


== tan ==
tan ken ona li tu:

anpa lawa li tan nasin pi wawa nasa tan kulupu jan Mon li tan nasin pi wawa nasa tan ma Asija pi open suno pi poka Sa.
ken la ona li tan nasin pi wawa nasa pi nasin sewi Intu, li tan lipu San (toki Sonko: 谶), jan Tan Sansan li kama jo e ona tan ma Palata.


== kulupu ona ==
kulupu tu wan ona li lon li ni:

anpa lawa kepeken misikeke(药降): anpa lawa meso, jan li kepeken ona tan ike anu lawa e jan ante kepeken misikeke tan pipi ike pi wawa nasa.
anpa lawa pi tawa sewi(飞降): anpa lawa pi meso ala, anpa lawa pi tawa sewi lawa la lawa pi jan pana pi ana lawa o weka tan sijelo.
anpa lawa kepeken ijo pi wawa nasa(鬼降): jan li lawa e jan ante kepeken ijo pi wawa nasa.
