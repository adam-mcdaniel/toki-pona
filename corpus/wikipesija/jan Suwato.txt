jan Suwato (toki Intonesija la: Suharto anu Soeharto [su'har.t…î]) li jan lawa nanpa wan pi ma Intonesija lon tenpo pini. jan Suwato li open lawa lon tenpo sike 1967 li pini lawa lon tenpo sike 1998. ona li pini lawa la, tenpo pi nasin ante li open.
tenpo pini wan la, ona li lawa ala la, jan Sukano li lawa. ona li pini lawa la, jan Pawalusin Jusu Apipi li lawa.
