jan Luwi Anson (toki Inli la: Louis Armstrong) anu nimi ale jan Luwi Tanije Anson (toki Inli la: Louis Daniel Armstrong) anu jan Sasamo (toki Inli la: Satchmo) anu jan Sasa (toki Inli la: Satch) anu jan Popa (toki Inli la: Pops) li jan pi kalama musi tan ma Mewika. ona li jan pi kalama musi Sasu li pona mute tawa jan mute.


== kalama musi ==
mute lili kalama musi tan jan Luwi Anson li:

kalama musi "ma li pona mute a" (toki Inli la: What a Wonderful World)
kalama musi "lon ale loje" (toki Inli la: La Vie en Rose)
kalama musi "jan Toli o toki a" (toki Inli la: Hello, Dolly!)
kalama musi "lon kipisi nasin anpa suno" (toki Inli la: On the Sunny Side of the Street)
kalama musi "lon lape la o toki e toki lili e mi" (toki Inli la: Dream a Little Dream of Me)
kalama musi "tenpo pi jan pi toki sewi tawa lon ni kama la" (toki Inli la: When the Saints Go Marching In)
kalama musi "tenpo pi sinpin sina li lukin pilin pona la" (toki Inli la: When You're Smiling)
