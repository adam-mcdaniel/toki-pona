toki Ikuli (toki Ikuli la: Iţkuîl /iθˈku.ɪl/) li toki sin tan jan San Kijata.
toki Ikuli la jan li ken toki kepeken sona jan mute kepeken nasin ni: jan li ken ala sona e ona kepeken nasin tu.


== kalama pi toki Ikuli ==
toki Ikuli li jo e kalama lili mute, li jo e nasin pi ike mute. tan ni la, jan ala li ken toki pona e ona.


== pona ==
toki ni la, jan li ken toki e sona mute kepeken toki lili.o lukin e ni:

Tram-mļöi hhâsmařpţuktôx.
ona lon toki pona:

sama ala sina la, la mi pilin e ni: ken la pini la kulupu ni pi nena kiwen suli li kama lili li kama weka lon pini.
o lukin kin:

Aukkras êqutta ogvëuļa tnou’elkwa pal-lši augwaikštülnàmbu.
toki pona:

pilin la meli pi len ala li tawa anpa pi pini ala lon leko mute pi kama sewi, nasin  tan tawa mute lon ale pi linja tu wan li lon monsi ona.ni li pali e sijelo awen pi nasa ala.o pilin lon pilin en sona en pona lukin.
toki Inli:

An imaginary representation of a nude woman in the midst of descending a staircase in a step-by-step series of tightly-integrated ambulatory bodily movements which combine into a three-dimensional wake behind her, forming a timeless, emergent whole to be considered intellectually, emotionally and aesthetically.
o lukin e ni kin:

Mmarnuqha  than-n  hwöeqtàdhûbüp  öeqtóu’adhiböp.
toki pona:

kulupu mama ali li pilin pona sama li pilin ike pi sama ala.
toki Inli:

All families are happy in the same way, while being unhappy in their own way.


== sona pi nimi lili ==
toki Ikuli la, nimi pi mute lili li jo e sona mute. nimi Ikuli wan li jo e sona pi nimi mute mute pi toki pona.


== sona tenpo pi toki Ikuli ==
toki Ikuli pi tenpo ni li tan tenpo mun nanpa tu pi tenpo sike 2023 li toki Ikuli nanpa tu tu. toki Ikuli pi tenpo pini li tan tenpo sike 2004 en 2007 en 2011.
lipu lawa pi toki Ikuli li lon ni.


== toki Ikuli sin ==
tenpo sike 2023 la, jan San Kijata li pini e pali li pana e toki Ikuli sin. toki Ikuli sin la, nimi ona li toki Malutawi (malëuţřait).
