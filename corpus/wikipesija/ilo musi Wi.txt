== nasin musi ==
jan li wile musi kepeken ilo ni la, ona o kepeken e ilo pi lawa e sitelen lon luka ona. nasin ante la, jan li ken kepeken e ilo pakala pi pana e kiwen e ilo pakala (ona li jo e nimi Zapper) tan ma Nijon (ona li jo e nimi Nunchuk) kin.
