ilo Towin (toki Sonko la: 抖音; Dǒuyīn) anu ilo Sito (toki Inli la: TikTok ) li lipu ilo pi sitelen tawa tan ma Sonko. mama pi ilo ni li kulupu Patensi (ByteDance). sitelen tawa mute li tan ilo Towin. tan ni, sitelen musi mute li tan ilo Towin.
ilo ni li open tan tenpo sike 2017. jan 2,000,000,000 li kepeken e ilo Towin. tan tenpo sike 2017 la, kulupu Patensi li esun e ilo Musikali (musical.ly). ilo Towin en ilo Musikali li wan tan ni.
tenpo ni la sijelo tu pi ilo Tuwin li lon li ilo Sito li ilo Towin.jan li ken lukin e ilo Towin lon ma Sonko, taso jan pi ma Sonko li ken ala lukin e ilo Sito tan lawa linluwi pi ma Sonko.jan pi ma ante li ken lukin e ilo Sito.
