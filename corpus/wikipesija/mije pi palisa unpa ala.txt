mije pi palisa unpa ala (toki Inli la: Eunuch, toki Elena majuna la: εὐνοῦχος) li mije li jo ala e palisa unpa.


== tenpo pini ==


=== ma Asija ===


==== ma Sonko ====
tan tenpo pi kulupu lawa mama So (toki Sonko la: 周朝) la, jan pi palisa unpa ala li kama lon ma Sonko.


=== ma Elopa ===


==== ma Italija ====
tan tenpo sike ale nanpa luka luka luka wan la, mije pi kalama musi li weka e palisa unpa ona tan awen pi kalama pona ona.
