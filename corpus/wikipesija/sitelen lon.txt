sitelen lon (toki Kanse la: photographie) la, jan li ken pali e sitelen pi ijo lon pi pali lon kepeken suno. sitelen ni li lukin sama ijo lon kepeken oko.
tenpo ni la, jan li ken kepeken wawa linja tawa sitelen lon. nasin ante mute pi pali pi sitelen lon li lon. jan Nisepo Nije (toki Kanse la: Nicéphore Niépce) li pali e sitelen lon nanpa wan lon tenpo sike nanpa 1826. taso, jan li jo e nasin sama lili tawa pana sitelen sama lili lon tenpo pini.
