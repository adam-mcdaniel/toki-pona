jan Sosija pi pana moli (toki Inli la: the Zodiac Killer) li nimi pi jan pi pana moli e jan mute. jan ala sona nimi lon pi ona. open tenpo sike nanpa 1968 en tenpo mun nanpa 12 tawa tenpo sike nanpa 1969 en tenpo mun nanpa 10 la, ona moli e jan lon poka tu tu wan lon poka ma tomo Sanpansiko. ona pana lipu pi toki muta tawa esun alasa pi ijo sin. mute lili toki ni li sitelen len. ona pini pana lipu ni lon tenpo sike nanpa 1974. lon lipu ni la, ona toki la, ona moli e jan 37. tenpo ni la, jan pi wawa lawa li alasa ona.
