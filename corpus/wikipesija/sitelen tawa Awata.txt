sitelen tawa Awata (toki Inli la: Avatar) li sitelen tawa tan tenpo sike nanpa 2009. sitelen tawa ni la, jan Sen Kamawin li jan lawa pi sitelen tawa li jan sitelen li jan poka tawa jan pana tawa jan ante pi sitelen tawa.


== insa pi sitelen tawa ==
sitelen tawa ni la tenpo li tenpo sike nanpa mute wan ale mute mute luka luka tu tu[nnp], jan li alasa e ijo pi lili mute lon mun Pantola, taso ni li ike tawa kulupu jan Nawi lon ni. kulupu jan Nawi li sama jan li jo e sona. jan li ante e ijo sijelo lili pi sona mama pi jan lon kulupu jan Nawi li wile toki tawa kulupu jan Nawi.
