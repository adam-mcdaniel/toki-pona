mi lon ma soweli (toki Inli la: Me at the Zoo) li sitelen tawa nanpa wan lon ilo Jutu li pali lon tenpo Sekon nanpa 52 pi tenpo Minu nanpa 31 pi tenpo Awa nanpa 3 pi tenpo suno nanpa 24 pi tenpo mun nanpa 4 pi tenpo sike nanpa 2005.jan Sa Kalin li pali e ona kepeken sijelo ''jawed'' lon ma soweli San Teko lon ma Kaliponija pi ma Mewika.sitelen tawa ni la jan Kalin li lon sinpin pi soweli suli mute.sitelen tawa ni li jo e tenpo Sekon luka luka luka tu tu li suli tawa linluwi.


== jo ==
jan Kalin li toki e ni lon toki Inli:

All right, so here we are in front of the elephants, the cool thing about these guys is that they have really, really, really long trunks, and that's, that's cool. And that's pretty much all there is to say.
