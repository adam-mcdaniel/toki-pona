ma Mijama (toki Mijama la: မြန်မာ) li ma lon ma Asija. ma Mijama li lon poka pi ma Tawi  li lon poka pi ma Lajo  li lon poka pi ma Sonko  li lon poka pi ma Palata  li lon poka pi ma Panla. tenpo ni la,ma Mijama li utala.

== kulupu Lowinja ==

kulupu Lowinja (toki Lowinja la: Ruáingga /ɾuájŋɡa/) li kulupu jan tan ma Mijama. jan Lowinja mute li weka tan ma Mijama tawa ma Panla, tan pali lawa pi ma Mijama. jan utala pi ma Mijama li moli li weka e jan Lowinja mute. jan mute pi ma ante li pilin ike tawa ona.
