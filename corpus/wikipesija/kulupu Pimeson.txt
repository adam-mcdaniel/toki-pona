kulupu Pimeson (toki Inli: Freemasonry) li kulupu mije.
tenpo sike ale nanpa 18 la, kulupu ni li kama tan kulupu jan pi pali kiwen.


== kulupu insa kulupu ==
insa kulupu Pimeson la, kulupu 3 li lon.

jan li wile kama jan pi kulupu Pimeson la, open la, jan ni li kama jan pi kulupu Enta Apensi (toki Inli: Entered Apprentice)
tu la, ona li ken kama jan pi kulupu Peloka (toki Inli: Fellowcraft)
pini la, ona li ken kama jan pi kulupu Mate Meson (toki Inli: Master Mason)
