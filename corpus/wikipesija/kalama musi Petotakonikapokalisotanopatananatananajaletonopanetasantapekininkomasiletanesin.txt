kalama musi Petotakonikapokalisotanopatananatananajaletonopanetasantapekininkomasiletanesin (toki inli la: PetroDragonic Apocalypse; or, Dawn of Eternal Night: An Annihilation of Planet Earth and the Beginning of Merciless Damnation, "moli suli tan akesi; anu, kama pi tenpo pimeja suli: pakala pi ma ale en kama pi ike suli") li kulupu kalama musi pi kulupu Kinkisatantalisawisa. 


== kalama musi ==
kulupu ni li jo e kalama musi ni:

sinpin tu pi sike
Converge ("kama wan")
Witchcraft ("wawa nasa")
Gila Monster ("akesi Kila")
sinpin wan pi sike tu
Dragon ("akesi suli seli")
Flamethrower ("ilo pi pana seli")
sinpin tu pi sike tu
Dawn of Eternal Night ("kama pi pimeja ale")


== toki ==
kalama musi ni li pana e toki musi ni:
nasin mani la ma li kama ike. kin la, sijelo pi kon ma li kama ike. ni la, jan pi wawa nasa li wile pona e ma. pali nasa ona li kama ike li suli e akesi. akesi ni li kama akesi suli li moli e jan ale e ma ale.
