musi Antateje (toki Inli la: UNDERTALE, toki pona la: toki anpa) li musi ilo tan jan Topi Pa (Toby Fox). ona li kama lon sike nanpa 2015.


== nasin musi ==
jan li lawa e jan lili Pisuki (toki Inli la: Frisk). ona li kama anpa tan nena Epa (toki Inli la: Mount Ebott) li kama lon ma anpa. "monsuta" li lon ma anpa li jan soweli li jan akesi li ijo ante mute. monsuta li ken ala weka tan ni: jan pi ma sewi li pali e selo wawa lon lupa.
monsuta li utala e jan la jan li ken moli e ona sama musi ante. taso musi ni la jan li ken moli ala li ken anpa e ona kepeken nasin pi utala ala.
