jan li pana e sitelen musi mute tawa jan ante tan kulupu pi ilo sona. sitelen ni li suli mute tawa nasin kulupu pi kulupu pi ilo sona. sitelen ni li tawa mute li lon tan lipu ilo mute. 
sitelen musi tan kulupu pi ilo sona li open tan kulupu pi tenpo sike nanpa 1990. sitelen musi open li tan lipu tawa linja (email). sitelen musi open li ni: sitelen pi jan lili tawa (Dancing Baby) en sitelen tawa pi soweli lili (Hampster Dance).
sitelen soweli pi kalama musi (lolcats) li open tan ilo Potan (4chan) tan kulupu pi tenpo sike nanpa 2000. lipu sitelen pi pilin ike (rage comics) en sitelen sinpin pi jan ike (trollface) li open tan tenpo ni.
sitelen musi jaki (dank memes) li lon tan kulupu pi tenpo sike nanpa 2010. sitelen musi pi tenpo ni li ni: kalama pi utala kute (ear rape) en sitelen pi pakala nasa mute (deep fried memes). sitelen ni li open tan ilo Wesi.
sitelen musi mute li open tan ilo Towin (抖音, anu ilo Sito, TikTok) tan kulupu pi tenpo sike nanpa 2020. sitelen musi pi tenpo ni li ni: sitelen Sipisi Tole (Skibidi Toilet) en musi Amanka. sitelen musi mute li lon tan tawa suli pi jaki Kolona.
