jan telo (toki Malasija la: Orang Minyak, "jan telo") li nasin pi wawa nasa lon toki musi wawa pi kulupu jan Mala. nasin pi wawa nasa ni la jan li ken tawa pi pini ala li ken tawa tomo tan lanpan pi ken ala tan unpa pi ken ala.


== jan unpa pi ken ala ==
toki jan la, jan telo li jan unpa li unpa e jan meli.toki jan la, jan telo li unpa pi ken ala e meli mute lon tenpo sike nanpa 2007. toki musi wawa la, jan telo li unpa e meli MMTT la jan telo li ken kama moli ala (ilmu kebal).


== o lukin ==
