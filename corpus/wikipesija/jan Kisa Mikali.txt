jan Salon Kisa Mikali (nimi suli Kisa Mikali) (toki Inli: Sharon Christa McAuliffe/Christa McAuliffe) li jan pi pana sona anu jan pi tawa lon ma weka. jan Mikali open lon kulupu ma Konko, Nuwansi, Mewika. jan Mikali li moli lon tomo tawa pi ma weka Salense. tawa tenpo moli, kulupu lawa pi jan pana sona en kulupu lawa pi jan tawa lon ma weka li pana jan Mikali e nimi pona suli. kiwen pi sama jan Makali li lon sinpin e tomo lawa pi ma Nuwansi. lon ni la kiwen ona li kiwen wan pi sama meli.
