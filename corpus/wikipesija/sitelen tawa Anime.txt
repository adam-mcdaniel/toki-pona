sitelen tawa Anime (toki Nijon la: anime, アニメ /aɲime/) li sitelen tawa pi lon ala tan ma Nijon. toki Nijon la, kon pi nimi "アニメ" li sitelen tawa ali pi lon ala. nimi Otaku (オタク /o̞ta̠kɯ̟ᵝ/) li tawa jan ni: ona li pilin pona tawa sitelen tawa pi ma Nijon. sitelen tawa pi lon ala pi ma Nijon li sama lipu Manka.
