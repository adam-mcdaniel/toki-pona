sitelen Kuntalija (toki Sinala la: කුණ්ඩලිය) li sitelen lili lon toki Sinala li sama sike pini lili lon toki Inli li kalama ona.ona li ෴.ona li sama  (monsuta) lon sitelen pona.


== tan ==
ken la,ona li tan ni:jan pi toki Sinala lon tenpo pini li kepeken kasi lipu tawa sitelen kepeken palisa sitelen kiwen.ni la,sitelen sike pini lili li ken pakala e kasi.ni la,jan li sitelen e sitelen pini kepeken sitelen ෴.ona li pakala e kasi lipu.ona li lon toki Sinala taso.


== tenpo ni ==
toki ilo Juniko la,nanpa sitelen ona li U+0DF4.ona li lon ala lipu mute pi toki Sinala,taso lipu lili li jo e ona.
