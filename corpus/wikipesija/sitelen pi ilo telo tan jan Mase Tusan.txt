sitelen "ilo telo" (toki Inli la: "Fountain") li sitelen kiwen tan jan Mase Tusan tan tenpo sike nanpa 1917. ona li ni: nimi "R. Mutt" lon  ilo telo jaki. jan Mase Tusan li pali e sitelen ni tan kulupu pi jan sitelen wan (toki Inli la: Society of Independent Artists). jan Ape Sikeli li sitelen suno e ilo telo. ona li open e sitelen ni lon lipu "jan pi lukin ala" (toki Inli la: The Blind Man). lipu ni li lipu pi nasin Tata. sitelen pi ilo telo li suli tawa nasin Awan Ka.
tenpo sike nanpa 2004 la, jan 500 tan ma Juke li toki e ni: sitelen ilo telo li sitelen suli nanpa wan pi kulupu pi tenpo sike mute nanpa 20.
