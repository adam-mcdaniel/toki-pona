jan Pesi Mekuwi (toki Inli la: Freddie Mercury) anu nimi pi tenpo ona kama lon li jan Palo Pusala (Farrokh Bulsara) li jan pi kalama musi tan ma Juke. ona li jan pi kalama musi Waka li pona mute tawa jan mute. jan Pesi Mekuwi li jan wan tan kulupu kalama Kuwin. kin ona li jan mije pi olin mije. jan Pesi Mekuwi li moli tan ike tawa li pakala e ilo awen pi sijelo jan lon tenpo sike nanpa 1991.


== kalama musi ==
mute lili kalama musi tan jan Pesi Mekuwi li:

kalama musi "jan lawa meli li moli e" (toki Inli: Killer Queen)
kalama musi "kalama musi mute ante pi ma Seki" (toki Inli: Bohemian Rhapsody)
kalama musi "jan pi tawa olin" (toki Inli: Somebody to Love)
kalama musi "mi mute nanpa wan" (toki Inli: We Are the Champions)
kalama musi "o pini ala mi lon tenpo ni" (toki Inli: Don't Stop Me Now)
kalama musi "ijo nasa lili li nimi olin" (toki Inli: Crazy Little Thing Called Love)
