ma Aki (toki Potuke la: Acre /ˈa.kɾi/) li ma kipisi pi ma Pasiju. ona li poka tawa ma Amasona tawa ma Kontonja tawa ma Pelu tawa ma Polipija. tenpo pini la ma Polipija li lawa e ma Aki.


== ma ==


=== nasin telo ===
nasin telo suli lon ma Aki li nasin telo Pulu (toki Potuke la: Rio Purus) li nasin telo Suluwa (toki Potuke la: Rio Juruá) li nasin telo Aki (toki Potuke la: Rio Acre).
